FAQs about hotels on Booking.com
How do I find cheap hotels on Booking.com?
There are a number of ways to find cheap hotels on Booking.com. You can filter hotels by price to only show those that match your budget, or sort by price to display the cheapest ones first. If you're a member of our Genius loyalty program, you can also enjoy discounted rates at select hotels and other properties.
Where can I find hotel deals on Booking.com?
We have a wide range of hotel deals and promotions throughout the year, all of which can be found on our deals page. If you're a member of our Genius loyalty program, you can also sign in to your account to see discounted rates at select hotels and other properties.
How many hotels are listed on Booking.com?
There are currently 21,936,990 hotel room listings on Booking.com, so you'll always be able to find the perfect hotel – wherever you're going!
How do I search for a hotel on Booking.com?
To search for a hotel on Booking.com, enter your destination, travel dates, and the number of people in your group. Don't know where or when you want to travel? Browse our site to get inspiration and find the perfect hotel that way instead.
How do I find cheap last-minute hotels on Booking.com?
To look for a cheap last-minute hotel on Booking.com, enter your upcoming dates to search for a hotel, then use the budget filters to find one within your price range.
Why should I trust Booking.com's hotel reviews?
You can trust Booking.com hotel reviews because guests can only leave a review after they stay at a hotel. This way you get verified reviews written by real Booking.com guests.
