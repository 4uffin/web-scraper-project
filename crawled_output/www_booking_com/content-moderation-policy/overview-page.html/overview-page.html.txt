That’s why we developed this set of guidelines that applies to the reviews, images, and listings uploaded by our guests and partners. We want to make sure that the content you engage with on our site is safe and helpful.When you post a review, image, or listing on Booking.com, it needs to comply with these guidelines. They form part of our broader set of policies, including our terms and conditions and privacy notices. If your content doesn’t comply, we’ll remove it or reach out to you about editing the comment to meet our guidelines.
