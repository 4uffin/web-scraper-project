Travellers
Stay safely
At Booking.com, we strive to help everyone experience the world safely. We have many processes in place to protect our guests, and to empower you to take control of your safety while you travel. Visit our traveller resource page to learn more about making your future stays go smoothly.

Host safely
Bringing peace of mind to our Booking.com Partners is a top priority. We help hosts feel confident about welcoming guests, and have processes in place to protect both you and your property. You can learn more about how to stay safe while hosting on our Partner resource page.
