About Isaac Rouse
Isaac Rouse is a versatile writer and editor with over a decade of experience covering games, tech, and entertainment. Currently a Gaming Journalist at Polygon, he’s best known for his in-depth interviews with major figures such as The Walking Dead and Invincible creator Robert Kirkman, Star Wars actor John Boyega, and legendary director Spike Lee. Outside the newsroom, Isaac is a beatmaker and YouTuber with a passion for RPGs, anime, movies, TV, and comics. When he’s not creating or consuming content, he’s likely digging into his varied collections, making music, or laughing along to stand-up comedy.
