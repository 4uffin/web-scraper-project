About Simone De Rochefort
Simone de Rochefort is a New York-based video creator, and has been writing, producing, and hosting videos for Polygon channel since 2016. Simone finds any way she can to use video games to talk about her love for architecture, design, and classic literature. Most recently for Polygon, she directed, alongside Clayton Ashley, the documentary The Great Game: The Making of Spycraft, which come out in 2024, and won the New York Videogame Critics' Circle Award for Games Journalism.
