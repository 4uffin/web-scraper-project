About Aimee Hart
Aimee Hart has been a games, entertainment, tech, and culture reporter and critic for over seven years. She has been featured in The Makers of Now series from Jezebel for her LGBTQ+ work within the gaming industry, as well as in Geoff Keighley's Future Class of 2021. She is based in the UK and, in her spare time, strives to apply her degree in Creative Writing with English Literature by writing as much as possible. Mostly about Star Wars.
