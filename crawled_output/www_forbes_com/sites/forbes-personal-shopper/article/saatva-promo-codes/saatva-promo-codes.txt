It’s a brand our sleep editors often feature in their mattress shopping guides—including their list of the best mattresses of 2025. It makes their pick for the best mattress for back pain (the Saatva Classic) as well as the best memory foam mattress for back pain (the Saatva Contour5) and the best adjustable bed for seniors (the Saatva Adjustable Base). If you’ve been meaning to upgrade your bed, I’ve compiled six Saatva promo codes and deals to help you save in the process.

Shop During Holiday Weekends
While you can almost always find Saatva coupons and deals, the brand typically offers its best discounts during holiday weekends. For example, over Labor Day you could save $400 on all of its mattresses. If you want to save more than the current $300 discount on beds, consider waiting until the long weekend in October to see if prices drop any further. It’s also possible that Saatva will offer some Prime Day deals during Prime Big Deal Days Oct. 7 and 8.
Start By Taking The Saatva Mattress Quiz
If you’re not sure which mattress you want, the brand’s quiz might be helpful. It has you answer some quick questions about your sleep style, firmness preferences and budget, then recommends the best model for your needs. It’s a simple way to narrow your options that’s especially helpful if you’re deciding between Saatva’s hybrid, memory foam or latex designs.
Check Out The Mattress And Bedding Bundles
Saatva offers two mattress and foundation bundles, as well as three bedding bundles that make it easy to revamp your bedroom in one fell swoop. With each bundle, you’ll be saving compared to the cost of adding each item to your cart individually. The Classic Bundle (which includes a Classic mattress, foundation, bed frame and mattress pad) starts at $1,578 for a twin with the current promotion.
Frequently Asked Questions About Saatva Promo Codes
Does Saatva Let You Stack Coupons?
No, Saatva doesn’t let you stack coupons.
What Is Saatva’s Return Policy?
Saatva offers a 365-night home trial for all of its mattresses. If you don’t like your new bed, you can return it for a full refund (minus a $99 transportation fee). Just contact Saatva customer service to start the return.
Does Saatva Offer Free Shipping?
Yes, Saatva offers free white-glove delivery with every mattress purchase, which includes in-room setup and optional removal of your old mattress.
Does Saatva Offer A Student Discount?
Yes. Students can save $225 on orders of $1,000 or more when they verify their student status using ID.me at checkout.
Does Saatva Offer A Military Discount?
Yes. Saatva offers a $225 discount to active-duty military members, veterans and government employees. You’ll need to verify your status at checkout through ID.me and make a purchase of $1,000 or more to qualify.
Does Saatva Have A Referral Program?
Yes. Saatva’s referral program gives you a $100 Visa gift card when a friend spends $1,000 or more with your link—and they’ll get $100 off their order, too.
