The brand makes some of our gear editors’ favorite shoes like the Clifton 10 (which they named the best shoes for standing all day), and the Stinson 7 (one of their picks for the best trail running shoes). Hoka sneakers typically cost upwards of $150, so I always recommend looking for Hoka coupons and deals before adding a pair to your cart. Here are the best offers from the brand right now.

Sign Up For A Hoka Rewards Membership
Signing up for a free Hoka membership is a good idea—especially if you shop the brand frequently. With the membership, you get free shipping on every order, 60-day free returns and invites to special events, like Hoka run clubs, and access to giveaways.
Read Our Hoka Shoe Shopping Guides
Our gear team tests dozens of sneakers every year to find the best models for various activities, and they’ve also created some brand-specific shopping guides. To help you narrow down the best model based on your lifestyle and the features you’re looking for, they’ve rounded up the best Hoka shoes for standing all day, the best Hoka running shoes and the best Hoka shoes for walking.These lists are based on firsthand testing insights, as well as input from experts like physical therapists and podiatrists. Speaking of which, we also have a list dedicated to the best Hoka shoes for plantar fasciitis, if that’s a concern for you.
Take Advantage Of Hoka’s Fly For 30 Guarantee
Hoka’s Fly For 30 Guarantee lets you try out your Hoka shoes for 30 days (or 60 days if you’re a rewards member). If you don’t like the fit or the feel, you can return them for a full refund or an exchange—even if they have signs of wear. Just keep in mind that final sale items don’t quality for the Fly For 30 Guarantee.
Use Hoka’s Shoe Finder Tool
If you’re not sure which sneaker to go with, check out the Hoka Shoe Finder. Through a series of questions, the Shoe Finder helps you narrow your search and populates three shoes that best suit your needs and preferences. It displays the shoes specs and explains notable key characteristics that relate to the questions you answered. You can visit the product pages to learn more about each style or have the results emailed to you to bookmark for later.
Frequently Asked Questions About Hoka Coupons
Does Hoka Let You Stack Coupons?
Hoka doesn’t have a clear policy on stacking coupons listed on its site, but shoppers report that you’re not usually able to combine them.
What Is Hoka’s Return Policy?
You can return any Hoka product within 30 days of purchase. Hoka rewards members get a 60-day return window. Navigate to the brand’s return center to process a return, exchange your purchase or track an item.
Does Hoka Offer Free Shipping?
Hoka offers free standard shipping on all purchases to its rewards members. If you’re not interested in signing up for a membership, non-members pay $8 for standard shipping, $20 for expedited, $30 for express and $75 for international shipping. You can read more about its shipping windows and fees here.
Does Hoka Offer A Student Discount?
No, Hoka does not currently offer a student discount in the United States.
Does Hoka Offer A Military Discount?
No, Hoka does not offer a military discount.
Does Hoka Have A Referral Program?
No, Hoka does not have a referral program.
Does Hoka Offer An Employee Discount?
Hoka reportedly offers its employees a 20% discount.
Where Can I Buy Hoka Shoes?
You can buy Hoka sneakers directly from Hoka (either in-store or online) or at retailers like Dick’s Sporting Goods, REI, Nordstrom and Fleet Feet. To avoid buying fake Hoka shoes, it’s best to shop directly at Hoka or at verified retail partners like the ones listed above. If you’re curious as to whether a seller is distributing authentic Hoka shoes, you can use the brand’s online store locator.
Are Hoka Shoes Good For Running?
Hoka shoes can be a great choice for running. After consulting with podiatrists and personally running hundreds of miles in Hoka sneakers, our gear editor narrowed down the best Hoka shoes for running. The Hoka Clifton 9 sneakers came out on top, ranking as the best Hoka running shoes overall for their breathable design and secure fit. The Bondi 9 was another top pick and a favorite for its ample cushioning and added comfort.
