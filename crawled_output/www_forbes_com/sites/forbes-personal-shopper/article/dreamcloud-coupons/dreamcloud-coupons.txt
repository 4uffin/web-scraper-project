The brand also makes their pick for the best hybrid mattress for couples, the DreamCloud Premier Hybrid. While its beds are already on the more affordable end of the spectrum, you can almost always find good DreamCloud coupons and deals to help you save further. Below, you’ll find six verified offers to take advantage of this week.

Take Advantage Of Bundle Deals
If you want to fully refresh your sleep setup, shopping DreamCloud’s bundle deals is an easy way to save in the process. The brand offers various combinations of bed frames, mattresses and bedding bundles that cost significantly less than if you bought all of the components separately.
Check Out The DreamCloud Luxe Mattress
If you’re not interested in the Classic Hybrid I mentioned above, our sleep team named the DreamCloud Luxe Hybrid Mattress one of the best mattress that won’t sag. The design was a favorite among our testers thanks to its solid edge support, exceptional motion isolation and cushiony, luxurious feel. Our editors also note that it’s an especially great pick for back and side sleepers.
Take Advantage Of DreamCloud’s 365-Night Trial
Ordering a mattress online without feeling it firsthand can seem like a gamble. But DreamCloud offers a 365-night trial, meaning you can sleep on the bed for a full year and still return it for a full refund if you decide it’s not the right fit for you.
Frequently Asked Questions About DreamCloud Coupons
Does DreamCloud Let You Stack Coupons?
No, DreamCloud doesn’t allow coupon stacking.
What Is DreamCloud’s Return Policy?
Thanks to DreamCloud’s 365-night trial, you can request a refund and donate your mattress within one year of purchase. Just keep in mind that you have to wait at least 30 days after delivery in order to initiate a return. To see if your mattress qualifies, check the specifics in DreamCloud’s return policy.
Other DreamCloud sleep products have a shorter return policy. Pillows, sheets and mattress protectors can be returned within 30 days. Meanwhile, there’s a 50-day return window for adjustable bases, platform beds and bed frames.
Does DreamCloud Offer Free Shipping?
Yes, DreamCloud offers free shipping on all orders within the contiguous United States.
Does DreamCloud Offer A Student Discount?
No, DreamCloud doesn’t currently offer a student discount.
Does DreamCloud Offer A Military Discount?
Yes. Active, veteran and reservist service members and their dependents are eligible for a 5% military discount. To qualify for the savings, you’ll need to verify your eligibility through SheerID.
Does DreamCloud Have A Referral Program?
Yes, DreamCloud has a refer a friend program for existing customers. When you share your personal link with friends and family, they can receive $100 off their mattress order. For every purchase made using your unique link, you’ll get a $100 Amazon gift card.
Does DreamCloud Offer An Employee Discount?
DreamCloud employees receive a free mattress and have access to a friends and family discount; however, the exact amount is not available online.
