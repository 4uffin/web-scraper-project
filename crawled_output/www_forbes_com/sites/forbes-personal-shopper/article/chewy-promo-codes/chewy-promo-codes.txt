It’s also featured in our guide to the best dog food delivery services for its value and convenience. I get my dog’s flea and tick prevention and treats delivered on repeat via Chewy Autoship—they’re cheaper than at other retailers this way and I love that they arrive at my door every month without a second thought. There’s also pretty much always a good assortment of Chewy promo codes and deals available to make spoiling your pet a little more affordable. Here are the best ones to take advantage of this week.

Sign Up For Chewy Autoship
Chewy Autoship lets you set up recurring deliveries for pet essentials, like food, treats, vitamins and more. You’ll save 35% on your first Autoship order and get 5% off all eligible items after that. You can also choose the frequency of your order, and change or cancel it at any time, making Autoship worth a look for most pet owners.
Consider Getting A Chewy+ Membership
For frequent Chewy shoppers, Chewy+ could be a worthwhile membership. The paid rewards membership costs $49 a year and gets you free shipping on all orders, exclusive deals and 5% back in rewards on every order (except gift cards and pet insurance plans). If you’re buying all your pet’s food, toys, treats and prescriptions at Chewy, the 5% back in rewards could add up fast. New members can try Chewy+ free for 30 days right now.
Make A Free Chewy Account
If you’re not quite ready to go all in on a Chewy+ membership, there are still benefits to having a free Chewy account. Sign up with your email to get access to the Autoship feature. You can also save your card and shipping information to make shopping easier in the future.
Opt For Dog Food Delivery
Chewy carries one of the broadest selections of pet food online, making it an easy choice if you want to set up recurring dog food deliveries and get those large, heavy bags delivered directly to your door. Our managing editor named it the best fast dog food delivery service after testing it firsthand. With a wide range of brands and price points, plus 24/7 customer support and quick shipping, it’s a reliable go-to for pet parents.
Frequently Asked Questions About Chewy Promo Codes
Does Chewy Let You Stack Coupons?
No, Chewy doesn’t allow you to stack multiple coupons.
What Is Chewy’s Return Policy?
You have 365 days to return or exchange most items. Some items, like prescription medications, are final sale unless your order is incorrect or damaged.
To start a return, call, message or email Chewy with your order number and you’ll receive a return shipping label. Then just attach the label to your package and drop it off at a FedEx store or authorized FedEx drop-off location.
Does Chewy Offer Free Shipping?
Yes, Chewy offers free standard shipping on all orders over $49. Orders under $49 ship for a flat rate of $4.95.
How Can I Get 35% Off At Chewy?
You can get 35% off when you place your first Chewy Autoship order. If you’re unfamiliar with Autoship, it’s a convenient way to schedule repeat deliveries from Chewy while saving on the pet items you consistently purchase. After saving 35% on your first order, you can save 5% on eligible items, get early access to popular releases and more.
What Is The Chewy WELCOME Promo Code?
If you’re a new Chewy customer, you can receive a $20 gift card when you place an order of $49 or more. Just enter the code WELCOME at checkout to apply the offer. Once your order ships, the gift card will automatically be added to your Chewy account.
Does Chewy Have A Referral Program?
No, Chewy doesn’t currently have a referral program.
Does Chewy Offer An Employee Discount?
Yes, Chewy employees get a 20% discount.
Does Chewy Offer A Student Discount?
No, there isn’t a student discount at this time.
Does Chewy Offer A Military Discount?
No, Chewy doesn’t offer a military discount.
