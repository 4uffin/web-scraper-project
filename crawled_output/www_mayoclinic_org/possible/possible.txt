1 & only.
Mayo Clinic is proud to be ranked No. 1 in Minnesota and Arizona, as well as the top hospital in the Jacksonville metro area, with more specialties ranked in the top three than any other hospital in the nation, according to U.S. News & World Report.
Cure, Connect, and Transform.
These three words are the foundation of our Bold. Forward. strategy—the driving force behind our transformation and delivery of healthcare. We are changing everything and creating new possibilities for patients by providing unparalleled team-based expertise for patients with complex or serious conditions, leveraging cutting-edge technology to connect with patients no matter where they are, and developing an innovative new digital data platform to deliver new knowledge and insights. The future of healthcare is here.
