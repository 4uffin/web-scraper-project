OfficesOverviewHomeHomeMedical Departments & CentersInternational Patient Services at Mayo ClinicMayo Clinic Appointment Services OfficesMayo Clinic Appointment Services OfficesTraveling internationally for medical care can be a complex process that varies from country to country. It may require travel visas, prepayments and translated medical records.
Mayo Clinic representatives, who speak your language, help international patients navigate the necessary steps to receiving care at one of our U.S. locations. They can serve as your single point of contact, from the moment you connect with them through the time you return home after visiting the U.S. They will take the time needed to understand your needs and assist you to tailor a travel plan based on your requirements.
Your Mayo Clinic representative’s goal is to make your visit to Mayo Clinic as efficient and as smooth as possible, so you can concentrate on your health and healing.
Your representative can help you:
Request an appointment and orient you to Mayo Clinic in the U.S.
Understand billing, insurance and expenses.
Discover travel and lodging options and understand the visa process.
Access translation services.
Follow-up on previous visits to Mayo Clinic and coordinate future visits.
Learn more about the services available in your country.

Independent referral facilitators in ChinaMayo Clinic works with independent referral organizations in China that facilitate the referral process for patients to all its campuses, including making travel arrangements. These recognized referral facilitators have worked with Mayo Clinic to establish a reliable process for assisting patients who wish to visit Mayo Clinic for healthcare services.
Find an independent referral facilitator.
Mayo Clinic Healthcare in LondonMayo Clinic Healthcare provides a variety of services in central London, including an Executive Health Program, comprehensive health assessments, specialty consultations, second opinions and diagnostic services outside the U.K.'s National Health Service. It also is a gateway to more than 7,300 Mayo Clinic experts in the U.S.
Contact Mayo Clinic Healthcare directly to make an appointment.
