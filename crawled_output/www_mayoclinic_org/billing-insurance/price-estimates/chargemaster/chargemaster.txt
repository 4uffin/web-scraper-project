Glossary of billing and insurance termsHomeHomeBilling and InsurancePrice estimatesCMS price transparencyCMS price transparencyThe Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) requires hospitals to make available online a listing of standard charges using a CMS adopted layout with charge information encoded in accordance with CMS technical specifications.
Mayo Clinic supports the intent of these regulations to educate patients on the cost of healthcare and patient financial responsibilities. Mayo Clinic has made a good faith effort to ensure the data in the price transparency files is true, accurate and complete.
To request a formal estimation of services, please contact us at 833-479-5483 (domestic patients) or 1-507-284-5063 (international patients). Office hours are 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Central time, Monday through Friday.
