HawkinsNo more Jeep hybrid pickup.Automotive News reports that Jeep is telling its suppliers to no longer expect a plug-in hybrid Gladiator pickup truck. It’s a curious move, considering the mid-sized truck is one of Jeep’s best-selling vehicles in the US, and its 4xe (pronounced “four-by-e”) hybrid lineup also remains quite popular.
