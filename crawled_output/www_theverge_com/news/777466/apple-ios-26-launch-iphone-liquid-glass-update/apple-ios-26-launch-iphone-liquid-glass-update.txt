Previously, she was a writer and editor at MUO.Apple is rolling out iOS 26 with a bunch of new features for the iPhone. The biggest change you’ll see upon installing the update is Apple’s Liquid Glass redesign, introducing translucent buttons, sliders, tabs, and other navigational elements across the iPhone’s interface.iOS 26 also includes Live Translation, an AI-powered feature that automatically translates texts in Messages, displays live captions on FaceTime, and provides spoken translations during phone calls or through your AirPods. You’ll also find a handy new feature that automatically answers calls from unknown numbers and allows you to screen text messages from people you might not know.Some other small but notable upgrades coming with iOS 26 include typing indicators and polls in group chats, as well as the ability to set a background image for different conversations.
