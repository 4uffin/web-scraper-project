About CNN Worldwide
CNN Worldwide is the most honored brand in cable news, reaching more individuals on television and online than any other cable news organization in the United States.Globally, people across the world can watch CNN International, which is widely distributed in over 200 countries and territories. CNN Digital is the #1 online news destination, with more unique visitors than any other news source. CNN’s award-winning portfolio includes non-scripted programming from CNN Original Series and CNN Films for broadcast, streaming and distribution across multiple platforms. CNN programming can be found on CNN, CNN International and CNN en Español channels, on the CNN Originals hub on discovery+, on Max and for pay TV subscription via CNN.com, CNN apps and cable operator platforms. Additionally, CNN Newsource is the world’s most extensively utilized news service partnering with over 1,000 local and international news organizations around the world. CNN is a division of Warner Bros Discovery.

CNN AI Editorial Guiding Principles
We are committed to the utmost journalistic integrity and accuracy
Any use of AI – generative or otherwise – must adhere to CNN’s values and must not undermine the trust audiences have in our journalism.
Accuracy and fair representation will remain our top priority. We will collaborate with organizations that have similar AI ethical values.
We are innovators and experimenters
We are a company with a legacy of using new technologies to advance our journalism.
We will invest in AI technology to improve our user experience, boost productivity, explore new creative opportunities, and combat misinformation and disinformation.
We have human oversight and guardrails
Our employees have oversight and responsibility for the AI systems and tools used, and the content they produce.
We undertake rigorous due diligence to evaluate our internally built tools and potential partners to ensure they adhere to both CNN and Warner Bros. Discovery’s AI principles.
We are transparent
We clearly signify to our users and audiences when they are seeing, hearing or reading AI content.
We are committed to fair depiction and inclusion
We are committed to fair depiction and inclusion of every kind of perspective when building or utilizing innovative technologies such as AI tools and services.
We believe the content created by our world-class journalists is unparalleled and valuable
AI presents new opportunities to both protect the intellectual property of our content and commercially support our business.
How We Use AI
Working with AI can help our journalists enhance our storytelling, streamline our processes and connect with audiences more effectively. That’s why CNN journalists are encouraged to use AI tools to assist with newsgathering, reporting and production, including but not limited to purposes such as research, translation, data analysis, graphics, banners, headlines and story summaries. Our journalists must maintain complete editorial control and review all our content before publication to ensure accuracy, objectivity, fairness and other adherence to our journalistic standards. Any exception to this practice will be rigorously tested and appropriately labeled.
