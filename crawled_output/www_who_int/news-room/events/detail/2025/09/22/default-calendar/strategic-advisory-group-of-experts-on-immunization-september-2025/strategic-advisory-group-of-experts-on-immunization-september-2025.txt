Strategic Advisory Group of Experts on Immunization (SAGE) - September 2025
22 – 25 September 2025
This meeting for the Strategic Advisory Group of Experts on Immunization (SAGE) will be held from Monday to ThursdayHow do I register ?Participation at the meeting is by invitation only. If you have not received an invitational email and would like to attend, please contact: sageexecsec@who.int.Where is the meeting?The meeting is planned to take place at WHO HQ. More details to follow.Where can I stay during the meeting?The meeting is in hybrid format; onsite participation is restricted.Meeting materialsDraft agendaDeclaration on interests (as 2 Sept)Additional meeting materials, such as a provisional list of participants and background documents, will be provided in due course.
