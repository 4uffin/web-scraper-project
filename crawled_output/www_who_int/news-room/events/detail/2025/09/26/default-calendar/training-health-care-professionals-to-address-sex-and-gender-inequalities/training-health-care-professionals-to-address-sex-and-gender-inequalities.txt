Training health care professionals to address sex and gender inequalities
Training health care professionals to address sex and gender inequalities
26 September 2025 12:30 – 13:15 CET
BackgroundThe 3rd edition of the global Women’s Health Conference 2025: Building Equity for Women’s Health Lifelong will take place in Paris from 24 to 26 September 2025 under the theme ‘Building Equity for Women’s Health Lifelong. The event will bring together researchers, health professionals and policymakers from around the world to discuss challenges and advances in women’s health.Charlotte Berquin, Localization Lead for the WHO Academy, will be speaking on the panel, ‘Training health care professionals to address sex and gender inequalities’, as part of the conference to share her experience in ensuring equity in the work of the Academy.Why Attend?The Women’s Health Conference 2025 is the perfect opportunity to explore recent advances in women’s health, understand global challenges, and propose innovative solutions to improve the health of women worldwide. You can:Access cutting-edge researchMeet renowned experts in women’s healthParticipate in interactive workshopsBe part of a global communityThis international conference is organized by Université Paris Cité, in partnership with the Global Centre for Asian Women’s Health (National University of Singapore), Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health, Stanford University School of Medicine and the iWISH Institute at Université Paris Cité.Event website: Womens Health Conference 2025 – Building Equality for Women's Health (3rd edition)How to JoinRegister to attend the conference here: Registration – Womens Health Conference 2025
