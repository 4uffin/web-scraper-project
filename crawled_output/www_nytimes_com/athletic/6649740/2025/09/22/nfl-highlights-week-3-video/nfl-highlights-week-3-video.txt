Our experts reacted to the biggest news, plays and performances from the day’s games, but we also want to make sure you get a chance to see all of the must-see moments from Sunday:Browns’ improbable comeback stuns PackersThe Browns’ victory over the Packers couldn’t be scripted as they blocked a go-ahead field goal and then kicked a 55-yard game-winner as time expired.Eagles rise to the occasionJordan Davis blocked a last-second field-goal attempt and returned it 66 yards for a touchdown, icing the Eagles’ 33-26 comeback over the Rams.Caleb Williams drives Bears over CowboysThe Bears claimed their first win of the season — 31-14 over the Cowboys — behind a 65-yard touchdown from Caleb Williams to rookie Luther Burden III.Chiefs get first win against 0-3 GiantsThe Chiefs struggled before winning their first game of the season, 22-9, avoiding their first 0-3 start since 2011. Meanwhile, Russell Wilson couldn’t have looked less like his Week 2 self as he went 18-for-32 for 160 yards and two interceptions, sparking chants of “We want Dart.”49ers get the final edgeQB Mac Jones led the way as the 49ers edged the Cardinals 16-15, thanks to Eddy Pineiro’s 35-yard field goal on the final play.Vikings add to Bengals’ painWithout quarterback Joe Burrow, the Bengals suffered the worst loss in franchise history, a 48-10 defeat to the Vikings marked by four turnovers.No Jayden Daniels, no problem for CommandersEven without their starting quarterback, the Commanders cruised to a 41-24 victory over the Raiders thanks to a strong team effort.Last-second FG lifts Chargers over BroncosThe Chargers are off to their first 3-0 start since 2002 after Cameron Dicker hit a 43-yard field goal as time expired to lift the Chargers over the Broncos 23-20.Rodgers makes history against PatriotsAaron Rodgers passed Brett Favre on the NFL’s all-time touchdown passes list, as the Patriots committed five turnovers, which proved decisive as the Steelers won 21-14.Bucs claim a special win over JetsThe Jets took a late lead on a blocked field goal returned for a touchdown, but the Buccaneers kicked a game-winner as time expired.Colts remain undefeatedJonathan Taylor rushed for three touchdowns as the Colts moved to 3-0 with a dominant 41-20 win over the Titans.Panthers rout NFC South rivalThe Panthers got their first win of the season, shutting out the Falcons 30-0, thanks to a dominant defensive performance — including returning a Michael Penix Jr. interception for a touchdown. Penix played easily his worst NFL game in his sixth career start in Atlanta and was subbed out for Cousins in the fourth quarter.Seahawks set the tone earlyThe Seahawks dominated the Saints 44-13 as Seattle ran up 38 first-half points and seemingly scored every time it touched the ball.Jacksonville forces Houston’s unravelingThe Jaguars’ defense forced three turnovers in the fourth quarter to secure a gritty 17-10 victory over the Texans.(Photo of Jalen Hurts: Mitchell Leff / Getty Images)
