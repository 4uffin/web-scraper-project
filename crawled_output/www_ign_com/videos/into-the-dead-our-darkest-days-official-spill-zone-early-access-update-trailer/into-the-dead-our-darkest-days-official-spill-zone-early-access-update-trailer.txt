Into the Dead: Our Darkest Days is a side-scrolling shelter survival game that transports players to Texas, 1980 at the height of summer during the outbreak of a deadly apocalypse. Lead a group of desperate survivors from shelter to shelter as they attempt to escape Walton City before it is overrun.
