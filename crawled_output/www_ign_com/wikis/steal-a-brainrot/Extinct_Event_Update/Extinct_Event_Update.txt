Extinct Event Chicleteira Ritual 5 More BrainrotsExtinct EventThe Extinct Event is a new limited-time event in Roblox Steal a Brainrot that gives you the chance to collect rare Extinct Brainrots. When the Extinct Event is live, one of three Extinct Brainrots will spawn every two hours.
