If you're itching to add Danny Boyle's latest to your physical media collection, you don't have to wait to do so. Walmart's got a 4K steelbook available to preorder right now for $48.49. IGN's Tom Jorgensen said that the film "finds Danny Boyle and Alex Garland mining the last two decades of cultural strife to build a timely coming-of-age story full of beauty and brutality in equal measure."
