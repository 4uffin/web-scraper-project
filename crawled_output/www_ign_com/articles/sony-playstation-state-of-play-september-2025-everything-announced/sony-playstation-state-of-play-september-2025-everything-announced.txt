There were a ton of reveals during the show, and we're here to break down the biggest ones right here to ensure you don't miss a thing. Marvel's Wolverine Gets a Gameplay Trailer, Fall 2026 Release Window, and Confirmation That Liam McIntyre Will Play LoganMarvel's Wolverine was the big finale for today's State of Play, and we not only got an extended look at VERY VIOLENT gameplay filled with blood and people being ripped apart, but we also got a Fall 2026 release window and the confirmation that Liam McIntyre will be playing Logan.The gameplay footage showed Logan both in and out of his very classic-looking suit, riding a motorcycle, traveling to different locations, encountering Mystique, Omega Red, and a Sentinel, and much more. While we may have to wait a bit for the game to out in the wild, Insomniac did confirm we will see the game again in a big way in Spring 2026.Saros Gets an Extended Gameplay Demo and March 2026 Release DateHousemarque's Saros kicked off today's State of Play, and we were treated to not only an extended first-look at gameplay of the follow-up to Returnal, but also a release date of March 20, 2026. This isn't a sequel, but a new game starring Rahul Kohli's Arjun Devraj, who finds himself on the shape-shifting planet known as Carcosa. The sun also plays a big part, and we hear a voice saying, "every time the sun dies, time stretches and tears." Oh, and weapons appear to be powered by the eclipse happening as well."We got to not only see the impressive tech and particles on display, but also a few abilities Devraj will get to utilize, including the Soltari Shield (defend and absorb projectiles) and Second Chance (Revive on First Death)There will be plenty of Holologs to find around Carcosa, and they will reveal more of the planet's past. The final shot of the demo was of a massive boss, but it cut away before we got to see Devraj fight them.First Look at the Battlefield 6 Campaign RevealedWhile we've already seen and played some multiplayer, EA has chosen this State of Play to reveal a first look at Battlefield 6's campaign. We don't have long to learn more about the campaign and multiplayer, however, as the game is set to be released on October 10.If you want more Battlefield 6, you can check out our hands-on preview of the game, which we said includes chaos, destruction, and surprising variety.Nioh 3 Was Given an Official Release DateNioh 3 will be released on PS5 on February 6, 2026. To learn more, you can check out all of our IGN FIrst coverage of the game, including our preview, where we said it looks set to level up from the first two games in the series. Zero Parades - For Dead Spies Is a New Spy Fantasy From Disco Elysium's ZA/UMZero Parades - For Dead Spies was revealed during today's State of Play and it looks to be a "narrative-first espionage RPG steeped in paranoia, surrealism, and a struggle for redemption." Players will take on the role of Hershel, a "brilliant yet unfortunate operant in a world where trust is scarce, betrayal is just around every corner, and failure is inevitable."This will be another cRPG like Disco Elysium, but it will come with some new features like dramatic encounters, failing forward and exertion, and conditioning.Zero Parades - For Dead Spies will be out on PS5 in 2026.Final Fantasy Tactics: The Ivalice Chronicles Gets a Gameplay Overview Trailer Ahead of Its Launch Next WeekWe here at IGN loved Final Fantasy Tactics: The Ivalice Chronicles, and the State of Play provided fans with a gameplay overview trailer to help with the wait for its release date of September 30.Crimson Desert Will Tell Kiff's Journey to Reunite the Greymanes Next MarchThe open-world action-adventure game Crimson Desert will be released on March 19, 2026, and the team celebrated that announcement with a story trailer. Crimson Desert stars Kliff, and we will control him as he works to reunite the Greymanes and rlly the forces of the continent of Pywel against a terrible evil.Chronoscript: The Endless End Tells the Tale of an Editor Trapped In a Manuscript of an Endless StoryChronoscript: The Endless End tells and interested tale of an editor trapped in a manuscript of an endless story who must write its final chapter. This 2D action-adventure game spans both the writer's mansion (which is rendered in 3D) and the manuscripts within (which are illustrated in 2D). Chronoscript: The Endless End will be released in 2026.Code Vein II Will Help Kick Off 2026Roughly seven years after the launch of Code Vein, its sequel - Code Vein II - will be released on January 30, 2026. PlayStation Plus Game Catalog October 2025 Lineup Announced, Headlined by Alan Wake 2Sony revealed the PlayStation Plus Game Catalog October 2025 lineup, and it looks to be headlined by Remedy's Alan Wake 2. Also joining the fun will be Cocoon and Goat Simulator, and all three will be available on October 7.As if that wasn't enough, The Last of Us Part 2 and its Remastered version will also be joining the catalog this month. Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 Is Headed to PlayStation 5Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024, which was previously exclusive to Xbox and PC, will be making its way to PS5 on December 8, 2025. Furthermore, PlayStation VR2 support is also in the works, although it seemingly won't be available at launch.Gran Turismo Celebrates 100 Million Units Sold and Gran Turismo 7 Gets a New UpdateAlongside celebrating the Gran Turismo series racing past 100 million units sold, Gran Turismo 7 players will be able to look forward to a Spec III Update this December that is the biggest update for the game yet, and it includes two new F1 race circuits, eight new vehicles, circuit experience, data logger feature, and more.Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds Is Getting a Mega Man Content in 2026Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds players can look forward to some pretty awesome Mega Man content in early 2026, as Mega Man and Proto Man will both join the roster of racers and will bring with them a course of a reimagined Dr. Wily's Castle from Mega Man 2. Deus Ex Remastered Is Out Early Next YearDeus Ex Remastered, an update to one of the most beloved games of all time, will be released on PS5 on Febraury 5, 2026.Halloween Video Game Gets Gameplay Reveal and Release DateIllFonic's Halloween, the stealth horror game based on the 1978 movie of the same name, was given a gameplay trailer and a release date of September 8, 2026, during the State of Play. Here's the official blurb on the game:Death comes to Haddonfield in Halloween, a chilling tribute to the iconic John Carpenter’s classic film. Survive or slay across the tense streets and dark corners of Haddonfield in this asymmetrical horror sandbox, taking on the role of either a desperate Civilian or the infamous Michael Myers himself. In each high-stakes cat-and-mouse match, every decision means life or death.As a Civilian, protect the townsfolk by guiding them to safety, scavenging for weapons and supplies, and finding a phone to call the police before it’s too late. Danger lurks around every corner, and survival depends on strategy, teamwork, and quick thinking. Meanwhile, Michael Myers stalks the shadows, using terrifying abilities like Shape Jump to move unseen across the map and strike without warning in a heart-pounding experience.Survival Action Roguelite Let It Die: Inferno Will Make Its Way to PS5 Before the End of 2025Take a trip to the "deepest, darkest depths of Hell" in Let It Die: Inferno, a survival action roguelite that stars an immortal explorer called a Raider who needs to uncover the ultra-energy within the Hell Gate. There will be a PvEvP mode within the Hell Gate and you can also "put your survival skills to the ultimate test in competitive matches against other players." Dynasty Warriors 3: Complete Edition Remastered Brings the PS2 Classic to PS5Dynasty Warriors 3 was released on PS2 in 2001, and on March 19, 2026 it will live again on PS5 in Dynasty Warriors 3: Complete Edition Remastered. This time around it will have improved graphics, content from the Xtreme Legends expansion, regined gameplay mechanics and UI, and more. Last Epoch: Oryboss Is Exciting New Expansion That Will Launch Alongside the Base Game on PS5 for the First TimeThe action-RPG Last Epoch will finally be bringing its "transformative skill trees, deterministic crafting, and community-driven development" to PS5 alongside its first major expansion, Last Epioch: Oryboss.Last Epoch supports solo play or allows players to bring three friends along with them, and the quest will send players across the world of Eterra and through time itself. There are five classes, each with three highly customizable masteries, and there are 120 unique skills to go along with them.The Seven Deadly Sins: Origin Tells an Original Story in the World of the Beloved Manga and AnimeLaunching on January 28, 2026, The Seven Deadly Sins: Origin will let fans of the iconic Japanese manga and anime series adventure through a free-to-play open-world RPG that can be enjoyed solo or with other players.The game will tell an original story and will put playrs in the shoes of Tristan, a "a young adventurer drawn into a multiversal crisis that threatens to unravel the world. With mysterious powers awakening within him and strange anomalies appearing across Britannia, Tristan sets out on a quest that will lead him through forgotten ruins, legendary battlefields, and rifts in space and time."DualSense Wireless Controller - God of War 20th Anniversary Limited Edition Announced to Celebrate Kratos' LegacyTo celebrate God of War's 20th anniversary, PlayStation is releasing a limited edition DualSense controller celebrating Kratos' legacy."Designed to evoke the legendary Ghost of Sparta’s ashen skin and scarlet tattoo, the controller pays homage to Kratos, who has taken on countless roles and endless challenges across Ancient Greece and the Nine Realms," the official description reads." The controller will be available for $84.99 and pre-orders begin on October 3, 2025 at 10am local time. Official PlayStation Wireless Speakers Announced by SonySony revealed its own branded wireless speakers called the Pulse Elevate, and they are designed for use with PS5 or a desktop PC gaming setup. Don't let that deter you though, as they can also work with you PlayStation Portal or even your smartphone if you so choose.They come as a pair - in both Midnight Black and White - and feature a built-in microphone with "AI-enhanced noise rejection" so you can even use these as voice chat.We don't have a price yet, but they will be out in 2026.Adam Bankhurst is a writer for IGN.
