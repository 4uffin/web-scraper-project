Learn more.A Charlie Brown Christmas Is Celebrating Its 60th Anniversary With a Zoetrope Vinyl Get it well before the holidays.Christmas may be a few months away, but Peanuts fans are getting a little early holiday treat to listen to. If you're a fan of the A Charlie Brown Christmas holiday special and its music, there's a new A Charlie Brown Christmas 60th Anniversary Zoetrope vinyl releasing on September 26. It's currently listed for $34.98, but Amazon has it for a slight discount, bringing it to $32.99. So if it's caught your eye and you're hoping to pick it up ahead of the holidays, now is your chance to do so.Preorder A Charlie Brown Christmas 60th Anniversary Zoetrope VinylOut September 26A Charlie Brown Christmas 60th Anniversary Zoetrope Vinyl6$34.98 save 6%$32.99 at AmazonWhat's so cool about this record is it's a two-sided vinyl with a zoetrope style that features classic scenes from the holiday special. As it spins, you'll be able to see the various scenes on the record move, which is an amazing element on top of the soundtrack itself to enjoy.
