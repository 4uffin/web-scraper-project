Not to be outdone by the Superman movie collection we highlighted this week, Bruce Wayne has his own Blu-Ray bonanza on offer.Amazon is offering the Batman 85th Anniversary Animated Collection in 4K for $79.99 - a steep 61% discount from the $204.99 list price. Across 10 movies, that’s $8 each.Batman 85th Anniversary Collection3$204.99 save 61%$79.99 at AmazonThis Batman Anniversary 4K Collection Is 61% OffThe 10 movies in this set include some excellent comic adaptations, like The Killing Joke and the iconic Year One, as well as Gotham By Gaslight and Hush.My favorite of the bunch has to be Batman: Mask of the Phantasm. This feature-length episode of the animated series packs everything you could want into a relatively short runtime.There’s Conroy’s Batman, genuine emotional challenges for him to overcome, a surprising new threat, and Mark Hamill’s Joker.Don’t skip Assault on Arkham, either.
