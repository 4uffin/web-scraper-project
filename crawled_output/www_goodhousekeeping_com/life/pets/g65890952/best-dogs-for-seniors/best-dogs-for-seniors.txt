Although they may not be able to talk, their loving eyes and wagging tails speak volumes in bringing constant affection and understanding to your life.It’s important to choose a companion for seniors carefully, to make sure a potential dog is well-behaved, easy to care for, and has easily-met exercise needs. With the help of the American Kennel Club, we’ve identified the best dogs for seniors that take these unique needs into account. From the best small dogs like the Dachshund to hypoallergenic dogs like the Bichon Frisé, these dogs for seniors are natural companions that love to snuggle and bring laughter and love. 1Cavalier King Charles SpanielBigandt_Photography//Getty ImagesCavaliers are classic companion dogs. They are known for their soulful eyes, even tempers, and silky coats, making them a beloved choice for families and seniors looking for a furry friend. They can be happy lapdogs, but they also enjoy moderate exercise.RELATED: 20 Best Dog Breeds for Families to Adopt2PugGCShutter//Getty ImagesThese funny animals can bring a lot of laughs with their outsized personalities and sometimes goofy looks. Their fur is soft like cotton, and they only bark to alert.

Dachshunds are known to be a bit stubborn, but their oversize personality will keep you endlessly entertained.6PomeranianThai Liang Lim//Getty ImagesThese tiny fluffballs (they weigh no more than seven pounds!) make lively lapdogs.
