Dijon mustard3 small Gala or Empire apples, cored and cut into 8 wedges1 tsp. fresh rosemary, chopped1/4 tsp. cayenne (ground red) pepper1 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oilsnipped chivesDirectionsStep 1In large bowl, combine ground meat, zucchini, bread crumbs, and 1/2 teaspoon each salt and pepper. Form into 4 meatloaves and place on foil-lined rimmed baking sheet; brush with Dijon.Step 2Toss apple wedges, rosemary, cayenne, olive oil, and pinch salt; arrange around meatloaves. Bake at 425 degrees F for 30 minutes or until loaves are cooked (165 degrees F). Garnish with chives.
