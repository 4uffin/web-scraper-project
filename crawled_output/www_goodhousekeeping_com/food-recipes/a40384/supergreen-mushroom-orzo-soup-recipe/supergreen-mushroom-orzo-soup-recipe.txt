Kosher salt1 1/4 cups celery1/2 cup shallots1/4 cup garlic8 cups vegetable or chicken broth3 cups broccoli3 cups sliced spinach1 cup sliced mushrooms1 cup orzobasil pestoDirectionsStep 1In an 8-quart saucepot, heat olive oil on medium. Add salt, celery, shallots, and garlic. Cook 8 minutes or until golden, stirring.Step 2Add broth and broccoli. Heat to simmering on high. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.Step 3Add spinach, mushrooms and orzo. Simmer 8 to 10 minutes or until starches and veggies are softened.Step 4Remove from heat. Stir in basil pesto to taste.
