10 minsTotal Time:
30 minsSavePrintThese curry-spiced salmon burgers give good ol' beef and turkey patties a run for their money.Read MoreAdvertisement - Continue Reading BelowIngredientsPrint3 green onions1 1/4 lb. salmon fillets, cut into chunks2 tsp. curry powder1/2 medium head red cabbageCooking spray1/2 green apple, cut into matchsticks2 Tbsp. cider vinegarbrioche bunsmayonnaiseDirectionsStep 1Slice green onions. In
food processor, pulse salmon fillets, half of onions, curry powder and 1/2 teaspoon salt until finely chopped.
Form into 4 (1/2-inch-thick)
patties. Freeze 10 minutes. Step 2Cut red
cabbage into 1-inch-thick
slices; spray with cooking
spray. Grill, covered, on
medium-high until lightly
charred, 3 minutes per side.
Spray patties with cooking
spray. Grill to desired doneness, 2 to 4 minutes per side. Step 3Slice cabbage and toss
with green apple, cider
vinegar, remaining onions and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Serve
burgers on brioche buns
with slaw and mayonnaise. Kitchen Tip: If using wild salmon, check for doneness sooner. It cooks faster, as it's leaner.Nutritional information (per serving):
