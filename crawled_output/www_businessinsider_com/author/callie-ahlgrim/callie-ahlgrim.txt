Callie Ahlgrim
Senior Pop Culture Writer
Callie Ahlgrim is a senior reporter at Business Insider who writes about pop culture, with a special focus on the music industry.As a cofounding member of BI's music vertical, Callie has covered a range of hot topics at the intersection of art, celebrity culture, and business: the impact of Beyoncé's blockbuster country album; the mythology of Frank Ocean; the promotional power of TikTok; the PR strategies of stars like Addison Rae, Chappell Roan, Diddy, and Katy Perry; the modern challenges of making money as a musician; and more.Callie is a two-time finalist at the LA Press Club Awards, most recently earning second place in "Commentary Analysis/Trend, Music, Online" for "Taylor Swift's new era."She graduated from Boston University with a Bachelor of Science in Journalism, a Bachelor of Arts in English, and a minor in Spanish. She previously wrote columns for Man Repeller and Elle magazine.
