ContentAll content from YouTube works with Ghost automatically via our OEmbed integration. All you have to do is paste a URL!When using videos in your content it’s important to use reliable video hosting with great performance so that your readers are always able to play your content. Generally speaking, self-hosting videos is very hard to do reliably - so it’s a much better idea to use a proper video provider and embed one of their players on your Ghost site.Fortunately you’ll find that Ghost is compatible with all of them, no matter which service you prefer! Here’s a quick guide for YouTube:Copy the URL of the videoGrab the URL of the video you’d like to embed into your post or pagePaste it into the Ghost editorWhen you paste it into the Ghost editor it’ll be automatically transformed into a rich embed of the video you selectedPublish your postThat’s all there is to it!
