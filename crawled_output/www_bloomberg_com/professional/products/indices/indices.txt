Innovative solutions for changing markets
Whether you’re looking for leading benchmarks or inventing the next wave of investment products, Bloomberg has the data, research and expertise you need.
Contact Us
Expertise to capture global investment opportunities
Bloomberg offers a broad and evolving suite of industry-flagship and bespoke indices across asset classes for benchmarking, asset allocation and product creation.
Unparalleled data and research
Bloomberg’s index team has access to an unparalleled breadth of trusted data, reliable technology and best-in-class research teams to bring new strategies to life.
Customization
Select from our suite of unbiased index products, including custom datasets, factors and styles. We actively work with index users on benchmark design, methodology, back-testing, selection and documentation of custom indices.
Cross-asset coverage
Our proven track record in creating strategic indices spans across asset classes, including fixed income, equity, commodity, multi-asset, currency, digital asset, reference rates, thematics and more.
Bloomberg Terminal Integration
Gain even more value from Bloomberg Indices by using the integrated analytics, research and index publications available on the Bloomberg Terminal.
Our index team and experts across Bloomberg collaborate to produce and maintain strategic, and often groundbreaking, benchmarks helping market participants address evolving needs.
