The TFFF is a new multilateral fund proposed by Brazil to support the conservation of endangered forests worldwide and the country is the first to commit money to it. By kicking off contributions, Lula hopes to spur other countries to follow suit ahead of the COP30 climate summit, which Brazil will host in November.
