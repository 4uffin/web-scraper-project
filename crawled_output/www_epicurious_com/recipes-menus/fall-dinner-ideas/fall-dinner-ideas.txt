Either way, as temperatures cool, we all need fresh fall dinner ideas. There’s no better way to celebrate the season than by embracing the autumnal bounty rolling into farmers markets and grocery stores near you. From tenderly roasted squash of all varieties and sweet potatoes to vibrant greens and crisp orchard fruits that brighten every bite, fall flavors don’t disappoint.That’s why we’ve collected some of our favorite fall recipes here. There are easy sheet-pan dinners, luscious soups, spicy curries, dinner-party-worthy salads, one-pot pastas, and so much more.
