Overview
Power groundbreaking AI with scalable and secure AI infrastructure
Purpose-built cloud infrastructure for AI of any size
Take advantage of Azure’s proven AI infrastructure to meet your specific AI needs, large or small. From training models to model distillation, fine tuning, and inference, Azure offers the right balance of performance, efficiency, and cost for your AI solutions.   Azure combines advanced compute, networking, and storage, to seamlessly deliver highly performant, secure, and scalable purpose-built AI Infrastructure to companies of all sizes.
Watch the video
Optimized at every layer—from silicon to systems
From silicon to software, our systems-approach optimizes every layer of the technology stack—giving you unparalleled AI performance.   Choose virtual machines optimized for AI. Eliminate bottlenecks with advanced networking and optimize performance with Azure Boost. Use responsible resources designed for energy efficiency with innovative cooling technologies.   With over 60 datacenter regions, more than any other cloud provider, Azure meets the needs of users worldwide.
Learn more
Reliable, secure, and responsible
Confidently build AI solutions on a trusted, proven platform. Azure provides scalable clusters with robust checkpointing for consistent, resilient performance across GPU-based virtual machines.   With hardware-rooted security, your data is safeguarded in use, in motion, and at rest. Your data is yours, never used to train other models.   Responsible AI guardrails integrated across the Azure AI stack and help prevent harmful content and promote ethical, human-centered interactions.
Learn more
Seamless integration for high-performance AI
Build, fine-tune, deploy, and scale AI fast and efficiently with seamless integration with Azure and third-party AI tools and services. Streamline data flow and resource sharing to maximize performance across AI workflows.   Innovate fast with Azure AI Foundry, powered by Azure AI infrastructure, with a broad selection of customizable models and AI services. Build and scale AI solutions in your preferred languages, frameworks, and environments seamlessly and with enhanced flexibility.
Learn more
