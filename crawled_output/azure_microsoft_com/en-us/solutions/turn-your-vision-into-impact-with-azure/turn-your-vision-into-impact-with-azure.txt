What is Azure?
Azure is a cloud platform with industry-leading AI and cloud services designed to help you bring new solutions to life. Use your preferred tools and frameworks to build, run, and manage applications across cloud, hybrid, and edge environments.
Learn more
02
What are the benefits of Azure?
Azure offers global enterprise scale, enhanced security, compliance, backup and recovery capabilities, as well as simplified management across multicloud and distributed environments and the flexibility to choose your preferred technology, tools, and languages. In addition, Azure provides access to industry-leading AI services, cloud infrastructure, and an intelligent data platform.
Learn more
03
What is cloud computing?
Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services—including servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics, and intelligence—over the internet to offer faster innovation, flexible resources, and economies of scale.
Learn more
04
Is Azure secure?
Yes, Azure is a highly secure cloud foundation managed by Microsoft—dedicated to security, data privacy, and responsible AI. Azure provides industry-leading, multilayered security across physical datacenters, infrastructure, and operations—with built-in resiliency features for high availability, disaster recovery, and backup.
Learn more
05
How do I learn to use Azure?
Start with Azure Fundamentals, an online training series exploring basic cloud concepts. Get a streamlined overview of Azure services and practical exercises to deploy your first service for free.
Learn more

How can I optimize my Azure workloads?
