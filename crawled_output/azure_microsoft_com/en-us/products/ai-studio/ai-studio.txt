01/
What is Azure AI Foundry (formerly Azure AI Studio)?
Azure AI Foundry is a flexible, secure, enterprise-grade AI platform that empowers enterprises, start-ups, and software development companies to bring AI apps and agents to production fast. Access a comprehensive catalog of models, agents, and tools to unlock your data and create innovative apps and experiences. Use developer tools you already know - GitHub, Visual Studio, and Copilot Studio - to build powerful solutions that can optimize call center performance, analyze data, improve product discovery, validate code, automate workflows, and more. Access Azure AI Foundry through a developer-friendly portal, unified SDK and API. Ship faster with quick-start templates. Deploy models to run from the cloud or local devices. Experiment with continuous feedback loops. Scale to deliver agents that optimize workflows with confidence. Easily manage all of your workloads in one place.
02/
What models are available in Azure AI Foundry?
Choose from over 11,000 foundational, open, task, and industry-specific models, including models from OpenAI, Microsoft, DeepSeek, xAI, Mistral, Black Forest Labs, Meta, Cohere, NVIDIA, and more. Modes span text, image, and audio tasks - support retrieval, summarization, classification, generation, reasoning, and multimodal use cases.
03/
Is Azure AI Foundry generally available?
Azure AI Foundry is generally available at ai.azure.com. Download the Azure AI Foundry SDK at aka.ms/aifoundrysdk.
04/
What is Azure AI Foundry Labs?
Azure AI Foundry Labs is a hub for developers, startups, and enterprises to explore groundbreaking innovations from research at Microsoft, including Muse, Aurora, ExACT, Magentic-One, MatterSim, OmniParser, TamGen, and other projects.
05/
Is Azure AI Foundry free?
The Azure AI Foundry platform is free to use and explore. Individual features customers access and consume are billed at their normal rates.
06/
Do I need an Azure account to use Azure AI Foundry?
You do not need an Azure account to explore Azure AI Foundry. However, before you can start building agents, you will need an Azure subscription.
07/
How is Azure AI Foundry billed?
Each service and product available in Azure AI Foundry has its own billing model and price.
