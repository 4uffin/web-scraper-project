What is the price for using Azure OpenAI?
Azure OpenAI offers both pay-as-you-go pricing and pricing based on provisioned throughput units (PTUs). Pay-as-you-go pricing allows you to pay for the resources you consume, making it flexible for variable workloads. PTUs, on the other hand, offer a predictable pricing model where you reserve and deploy a specific amount of model processing capacity. This model is ideal for workloads with consistent or predictable usage patterns, providing stability and cost control.
See Azure OpenAI pricing
