Frequently asked questions

01/
What are Azure AI services?
Azure AI services is a collection of Azure products that supports investment in making easy-to-use AI. This collection includes Azure OpenAI, AI Search, AI Speech, and Content Safety, among other products and services.
Learn more
02/
Is Azure AI services the same as Azure Cognitive Services?
Yes, Azure Cognitive Services was the original name for this product collection. We updated the name to Azure AI services to better match our compendium of products.
03/
Does Azure AI services cost anything?
Each service has pricing that is customizable to the application or use case.
Learn more
04/
What is the difference between Azure machine learning and Azure AI services?
Azure AI services have a lower barrier to entry and are designed for all types of users, while Azure machine learning is primarily aimed toward data scientists who are looking to design a more bespoke solution.
