FAQsDoes Bluetti offer free shipping?Yes, all orders that are shipped to the contiguous USA come with free shipping as standard. Orders usually arrive in 8 - 11 working days and tend to be shipped via FEDEX or UPS. You can double-check that you order does not incur any shipping fees at the checkout before you place your order.What are Bluetti Bucks?Bluetti Bucks are the brand's membership program currency, and are earned by registered members with every purchase they make. You'll earn 1 Bluetti Buck for every $1 you spend, and the currency can be redeemed on a range of branded merchandise, gift cards and discount coupons through the dedicated members page. You can also earn additional Bluetti Bucks through other actions such as completing your profile, following the brand on social media and leaving product reviews.Does Bluetti price match?Yes - as well as exclusive discounts, Bluetti Bucks and free shipping, Bluetti members also get access to the brand's price match program. This allows you to notify the brand if you see one of their devices sold for cheaper elsewhere, and so long as you do so within 30 days of making your purchase, you'll be refunded the difference.What is the Bluetti returns policy?Should you want to send an item back to Bluetti, you can do so for a refund, so long as you do so within 30 days of making your purchase. You'll need to let the brand know of your decision before you send your item, and you'll also have to either arrange your own return shipping, or use Bluetti's return shipping service (in which case the cost will be deducted from your refund total).Bluetti Saving TipsCheck Bluetti salesBluetti runs a number of sales throughout the year, including the likes of its home backup sale, which can knock up to $3500 off the price of selected products. We tend to see sales throughout the year, with the biggest and best around key holiday periods such as Black Friday.Refer a friendYou can refer a friend to Bluetti to secure them a discount and get sent your own code for savings on your next order. If you know someone who'd like the brand, just provide your name and email address. Bluetti will send a discount code to your inbox - pass it on to your friend, who will get 8% off at the checkout, and once they've made a purchase, you'll be sent a code for 5% off your own order as a thank you.Become a Bluetti memberBluetti members get a number of perks that non-members don't get that can help you save on orders. First, members are automatically signed up to the mailing list, meaning they'll be sent news on products and promotions, as well as a selection of exclusive deals, as soon as they're available. Members will also receive gifts with select purchases, as well as free & fast delivery on all orders. Finally, members earn points which can be redeemed for branded merch & accessories. Just provide your chosen email address through the online form to sign up free of charge.Get a government discountYou can save 5% on your orders by verifying as a government employee with a valid GovX account. Professions eligible for this saving include members of the military, both active & retired, members of military families, and first responders such as police, fire or healthcare workers.Trade in your old devicesIf you already own a Bluetti device and no longer use it, or are looking to upgrade to a better model, you can make use of the brand's trade-in scheme to exchange it for some store credit. Bluetti offers up to $1300 for selected models, giving you a significant sum that you can spend at the checkout when you place your next order. About Bluetti
Bluetti is a Chinese electronics company that produces portable power generators including solar-powered models and energy storage devices. The brand first entered the market in 2009 before expanding to the United States in 2024. Its range is tailored towards those looking for independent home energy systems, helping you live off-grid or set up a backup power supply for use in emergencies. As well as power systems, the brand also sells a number of useful accessories such as chargers, cables and adapters. If you're looking to buy some hardware for your home energy setup, see if we can help you save with the latest Bluetti discount codes.
