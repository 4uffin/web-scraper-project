About Origin PC
Origin PC was founded in 2009 by former Alienware executives Richard Cary, Hector Penton, and Kevin Wasielewski, who came together in Miami, Florida looking to revitalize their love for building machines. You'll find everything from gaming desktops to professional laptops in their range. Origin PC was bought by leading PC components brand Corsair back in 2019, and still serves a wide variety of customers including casual users and enthusiasts today.
