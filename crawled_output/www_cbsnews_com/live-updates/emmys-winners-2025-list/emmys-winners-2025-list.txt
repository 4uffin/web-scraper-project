"The Pitt," "The Studio," and "Adolescence" win big at Emmy Awards
05:21
What to know about the 2025 Emmy AwardsThe 77th Annual Primetime Emmy Awards were held Sunday night to honor the best of television from the past year.Apple TV+'s "Severance" went into the night with the most nominations, 27, while Apple TV+'s "The Studio" tied a record for a comedy series with 23 nominations. "The Studio" took home honors for best comedy series, best writing for a comedy and best directing for a comedy."The Pitt" won outstanding drama series, and actors Noah Wyle and Katherine LaNasa each won their first ever Emmys for their roles in the show.Netflix's "Adolescence" also won big, earning six awards, including best limited series.Comedian Nate Bargatze hosted the Emmys this year for the first time. He also received two nominations for his variety special "Your Friend, Nate Bargatze."Check out the full list of winners and nominees below.
