Iraqi president Abdul Rashid on ISIS threat, U.S. military presence and Gaza war
During a brief exchange at the United Nations with President Trump, Iraqi President Abdul Rashid expressed his commitment to engaging in the rising tensions in the Middle East. During his address to the General Assembly, Rashid joined a chorus of world leaders endorsing a Palestinian state -- a view both Israel and the U.S. oppose. "CBS Evening News" co-anchor Maurice DuBois sat down with Rashid.
