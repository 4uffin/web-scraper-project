New book explores Tom Brady and Bill Belichick's dynasty years
Author Gary Myers, who has covered the NFL for more than four decades, talks about his latest book "Brady vs. Belichick: The Dynasty Debate." The book takes a closer look at how Tom Brady and Bill Belichick built one of the most successful player-coach relationships in sports, winning six Super Bowls during their time with the New England Patriots.
