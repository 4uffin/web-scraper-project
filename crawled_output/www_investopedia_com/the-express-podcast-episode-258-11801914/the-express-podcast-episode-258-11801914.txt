Over-Concentrated or Over-Diversified?

With the stock market near record highs again, and nearly every valuation metric pointing to extremes, are we at the precipice of an “irrational exuberance” moment, or just riding this bull through an AI expansion that could last for years? We go inside the charts with Ian Dunlap, aka “The Master Investor,” and founder of the Red Panda Stock Club.
Also, ETFs are now surpassing single stocks, begging the question: are we over-diversified in a world where stocks like Nvidia dominate index funds? We also delve into what to watch this week as the labor market faces its latest test.
