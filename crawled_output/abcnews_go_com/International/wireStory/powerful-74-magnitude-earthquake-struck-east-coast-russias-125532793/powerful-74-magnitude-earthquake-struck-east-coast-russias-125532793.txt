OffOnStream onPowerful earthquake strikes near the east coast of Russia’s Kamchatka regionA powerful 7.4 magnitude earthquake has struck near the east coast of Russia’s Kamchatka regionByThe Associated PressSeptember 12, 2025, 11:27 PM1:53MOSCOW -- A powerful 7.4 magnitude earthquake struck early Saturday near the east coast of Russia’s Kamchatka region, the U.S. Geological Survey reported.The quake’s epicenter was 111.7 kilometers (69.3 miles) east of Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky, and had a depth of 39 kms, according to the USGS.There were no immediate reports of injuries or major damages. The Pacific Tsunami Warning System briefly said there was a threat of a possible tsunami from the earthquake but later dropped the threat from its website.
