(Photo by Richard Shotwell/Invision/AP)The Associated PressLOS ANGELES -- LOS ANGELES (AP) — “The Studio” made history at the 77th Primetime Emmy Awards by winning 13 awards, becoming the most awarded comedy series in a single season. doctor.“Adolescence” won six awards in the limited series categories, including best supporting actor for 15-year-old Owen Cooper. “Severance” entered the ceremony as the top overall nominee and ended
