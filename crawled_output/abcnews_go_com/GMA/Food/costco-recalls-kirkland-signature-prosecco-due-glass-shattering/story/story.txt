UIG via Getty Images, FILESparkling wine drinkers are being urged not to pop bottles of Kirkland Signature Prosecco that was recalled in 12 states.Costco Wholesale announced in a letter to its members that it has recalled Kirkland Signature Prosecco Valdobbiadene"There is a risk of unopened bottles shattering, even when not handled or in use," the recall stated. "If you have an unopened bottle, do not open it.
