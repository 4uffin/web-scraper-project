(AP Photo/Jeff Chiu, file)The Associated PressBERKELEY, Calif. -- An earthquake with a magnitude of 4.3 woke up a number of people with a sharp jolt in the San Francisco Bay Area early Monday, according to the United States Geological Survey.The earthquake was just east-southeast of Berkeley, the survey said. It happened shortly before 3 a.m. PDT.People posted online that they felt their homes shake.
