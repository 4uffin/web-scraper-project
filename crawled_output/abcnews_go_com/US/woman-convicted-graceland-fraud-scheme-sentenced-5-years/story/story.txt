at the sentencing hearing.Findley has been in custody since her arrest in August 2024.  In the plea agreement, Findley admitted the government's factual basis in the case is "true and accurate," and that had the case gone to trial the evidence would have established her guilt beyond a reasonable doubt.As part of a plea deal, prosecutors agreed to dismiss one count of aggravated identity theft
