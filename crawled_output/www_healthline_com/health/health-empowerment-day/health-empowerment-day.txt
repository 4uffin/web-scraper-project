This mission starts with access to accurate and accessible information to help empower our readers to make the best decisions to support their health journeys. That’s why this September, we’re partnering with our sister brand, Healthgrades, to spread awareness about Health Empowerment Day. Health Empowerment Day is a new annual awareness moment created by Healthgrades to inspire and support people, especially those living with chronic conditions, to feel more confident, informed, and in control of their healthcare journey.At Healthline, we also believe that healthcare looks different for everyone.
