A public memorial service for the conservative political activist Charlie Kirk drew tens of thousands of people to State Farm Stadium in Glendale, Arizona, on Sunday.
Bloomberg / Getty Images
President Trump spoke at the service, heralding Kirk as a "martyr for American freedom." Other speakers and attendees included JD Vance, Elon Musk, RFK Jr., Eric Trump, Laura Loomer, Tulsi Gabbard, Dr. Oz, Tucker Carlson, Pete Hegseth, Stephen Miller, and more.
