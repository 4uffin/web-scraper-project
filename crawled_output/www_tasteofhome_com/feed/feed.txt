<p>Soup season just got the best upgrade ever.</p>
<p>The post <a href="https://www.tasteofhome.com/article/panera-black-bean-soup-2025/">Panera Brought Back a Beloved Soup Just in Time for Fall</a> appeared first on <a href="https://www.tasteofhome.com">Taste of Home</a>.</p>
<p>I would consider myself a die-hard Panera fan and have been ever since I had my first Sierra Turkey sandwich. I mean, I&rsquo;ve even tested the chain&rsquo;s <a href="https://www.tasteofhome.com/article/panera-bread-mac-and-cheese-lip-balm-2025/" data-name="www.tasteofhome.com/article/panera-bread-mac-and-cheese-lip-balm-2025/" data-module="content engagement" data-position="embedded links">mac and cheese lip balm</a>! Although Panera took my beloved Sierra Turkey off the menu, I will ask them to bring it back every chance I get. Here goes: Panera, please listen to my plea!</p><p>There are fewer fast-casual restaurant chains that I have spent more time in than a Panera Bread. When I traveled a lot for work, Panera was always my go-to stop for lunch and a reliable Wi-Fi connection. Sometimes, I wonder just how many You Pick Twos I&rsquo;ve eaten in my life&mdash;or how many different combinations.</p><p>So it pleases me greatly that Panera has brought back one of its best soups <em>ever</em> this fall.</p><h2>What soup is back at Panera?</h2><p><img decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-2186714 size-large" src="https://www.tasteofhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/Panera-Brought-Back-a-Beloved-Soup_AdobeStock_44294330_ATedit-e1758818960677.jpg?fit=680%2C454" alt="" width="700" height="467" data-recalc-dims="1" title="Panera Brought Back A Beloved Soup Adobestock 44294330 Atedit" data-has-syndication-rights="1" data-portal-copyright="Bert Folsom/stock.adobe.com"></p><p>It&rsquo;s time for the triumphant return of Panera&rsquo;s black bean soup. After a full&nbsp;<em>seven year&nbsp;</em>hiatus, the black bean soup is finally back on the menu. This has long been lauded by fans as Panera&rsquo;s best soup, so it was puzzling when it was discontinued. Even though I have a soft spot for the <a href="https://www.tasteofhome.com/recipes/cheesy-broccoli-soup-in-a-bread-bowl/" data-name="www.tasteofhome.com/recipes/cheesy-broccoli-soup-in-a-bread-bowl/" data-module="content engagement" data-position="embedded links">broccoli cheddar in a bread bowl</a>, I do agree that the black bean soup is the best offering. Plus, unlike Panera&rsquo;s other soups, the black bean soup is vegetarian.</p><p>The black bean soup is filled with beans, of course, both black and lima. It&rsquo;s rounded out with some other veggies and aromatics like onions and garlic for a hearty bowl. It also has lemon juice, which I think is essential to cutting through all those heavy flavors and adding a bit of brightness.</p><h2>How long will the black bean soup be back?</h2><p>The soup is available at Panera locations nationwide right now. As for how long it will be available? That&rsquo;s a little tricker to tell. Right now, it remains unclear as to whether this is back permanently, or if it&rsquo;s just a limited-time seasonal offering. Regardless of if this is back for a few months or forever, I take this as a sign that Panera is moving in the right direction by listening to its fans. Now, please, bring back the Sierra Turkey sandwich!</p><h2>Related:</h2><ul><li><a href="https://www.tasteofhome.com/collection/soups-to-keep-you-warm-this-fall/?int_campaign=tmb_trend_recirc&amp;int_source=direct&amp;int_medium=tmb.com&amp;int_placement=single_card" data-name="www.tasteofhome.com/collection/soups-to-keep-you-warm-this-fall/?int_campaign=tmb_trend_recirc&amp;int_source=direct&amp;int_medium=tmb.com&amp;int_placement=single_card" data-module="content engagement" data-position="embedded links">Fall Soup Recipes to Keep You Warm and Toasty</a></li><li><a href="https://www.tasteofhome.com/collection/panera-bread-copycat-recipes/?int_campaign=tmb_trend_recirc&amp;int_source=direct&amp;int_medium=tmb.com&amp;int_placement=single_card" data-name="www.tasteofhome.com/collection/panera-bread-copycat-recipes/?int_campaign=tmb_trend_recirc&amp;int_source=direct&amp;int_medium=tmb.com&amp;int_placement=single_card" data-module="content engagement" data-position="embedded links">Copycat Panera Bread Recipes</a></li><li><a href="https://www.tasteofhome.com/collection/panera-bread/?int_campaign=tmb_trend_recirc&amp;int_source=direct&amp;int_medium=tmb.com&amp;int_placement=single_card" data-name="www.tasteofhome.com/collection/panera-bread/?int_campaign=tmb_trend_recirc&amp;int_source=direct&amp;int_medium=tmb.com&amp;int_placement=single_card" data-module="content engagement" data-position="embedded links">10 Secrets You Might Not Know About Panera Bread</a></li></ul><p>The post <a href="https://www.tasteofhome.com/article/panera-black-bean-soup-2025/">Panera Brought Back a Beloved Soup Just in Time for Fall</a> appeared first on <a href="https://www.tasteofhome.com">Taste of Home</a>.</p>

This maple bourbon banana bread brings all the extra fall drama with it. It's rich and moist, infused with warm bourbon and sweet maple syrup and sprinkled with melty pockets of chocolate toffee that caramelize as it bakes. Every bite is a perfect storm of cozy, boozy and indulgent, making it just as welcome at a brunch table as it is for a late-night treat. —Elise Jesse, Cincinnati, Ohio

There's no better way to enjoy a normal cup of coffee than by turning it into jelly! Vietnamese coffee is known to be strong and is typically served as a light dessert, but it can be served in any shape or form. This unique spin turns it into Vietnamese coffee jelly with a drizzle of condensed milk on top. —Tam To, Los Angeles, California

<p>One of my absolute favorite comfort foods is macaroni and cheese. When I&rsquo;m feeling ambitious, I&rsquo;ll make it from scratch, using a roux as the base for a smooth and creamy <a href="https://www.tasteofhome.com/recipes/cheddar-cheese-sauce/" data-name="www.tasteofhome.com/recipes/cheddar-cheese-sauce/" data-module="content engagement" data-position="embedded links">cheese sauce</a>. I visited the <a href="https://www.tillamook.com/visit-us/creamery" data-name="www.tillamook.com/visit-us/creamery" data-module="content engagement" data-position="embedded links" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow">Tillamook Creamery</a> in Tillamook, Oregon, as well as one of their dairy farms. What can I say, I&rsquo;m <em>very</em> thorough!</p><h2>Cheddar vs. white cheddar: What&rsquo;s the difference?</h2><p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-2184882 size-large" src="https://www.tasteofhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/White-Cheddar-vs-Cheddar_IMG_9681_Hayley-Schueneman-for-taste-of-home.jpg?fit=680%2C995" alt="" width="700" height="1024" data-recalc-dims="1" title="White Cheddar Vs Cheddar Img 9681 Hayley Schueneman For Taste Of Home" data-has-syndication-rights="1" data-portal-copyright="HAYLEY SCHUENEMAN FOR TASTE OF HOME"></p><p>If you want to be cheeky and say, &ldquo;Well, they&rsquo;re different colors,&rdquo; you&rsquo;re actually about halfway to the truth. As Jill Allen, Director of Product Excellence at Tillamook, pointed out to me, all cheddar is naturally white. That&rsquo;s because cheese is made from milk, which is white. But it&rsquo;s not necessarily <em>uniformly</em> white.</p><p>From pale, buttery cream shades with just a tinge of yellow to bright, almost cool-toned white shades, milk can be different colors from the white spectrum. Jill explained that there are several factors can that influence the color of a cow&rsquo;s milk, including the time of year, the temperature and the cow&rsquo;s diet. Even from cow to cow there can be a lot of variation.</p><p>In order to make blocks of cheddar look more uniform, a coloring agent called annatto is added to the milk as it begins its journey to becoming cheese.</p><h2>What is annatto?</h2><p><img loading="lazy" decoding="async" class="alignnone wp-image-2184883 size-large" src="https://www.tasteofhome.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/White-Cheddar-vs-Cheddar_IMG_9720_Hayley-Schueneman-for-taste-of-home.jpg?fit=680%2C995" alt="" width="700" height="1024" data-recalc-dims="1" title="White Cheddar Vs Cheddar Img 9720 Hayley Schueneman For Taste Of Home" data-has-syndication-rights="1" data-portal-copyright="HAYLEY SCHUENEMAN FOR TASTE OF HOME"></p><p>Annatto is made from the seeds of the achiote tree, which is indigenous to Central and South America, Mexico, and the Caribbean. The annatto seeds can be ground into a powder and used as an edible dye. It&rsquo;s in many products, from smoked fish to lipstick to, of course, cheese.</p><p>I first learned about annatto when I was working at Whole Foods. It was one of the natural dyes that the bakery department used to give frosting an orange tint. In fact, A-Sha&rsquo;s spice-loving fans inspired us to partner with Hot Ones to create a fiery lineup of increasingly spicy and flavorful ramen noodle kits that turn up the heat with every bite. Balance your plate</h3><p><b>TOH:</b> What kinds of meals or dishes have a good balance of flavors that incorporate spice, but don&rsquo;t overpower it?</p><p><strong>YC:</strong> Dishes that balance sweet, salty, sour and spicy are ideal. That&rsquo;s something we strive for with A-Sha&rsquo;s spicy lineup: bold but balanced.</p><p><strong>TOH: </strong>Any final thoughts on spice?</p><p><strong>YC: </strong>Spice is meant to elevate, not punish.
