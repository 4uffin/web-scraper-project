Virginia Beach, VA
School: Old Dominion University
Expertise: Recipe Development; Health & Wellness; Grilling & Smoking
Follow:
Tyler Smith
30K followers across TikTok, Instagram, Facebook and YouTube as food influencer @girldadskitchen
Creator of a viral sandwich with 2.2 million views on TikTok
Experience
Tyler is a home cook with two hungry daughters. In 2024, he began to share his recipes as @girldadskitchen on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube and Facebook. In his first year as a food influencer, Tyler gained over 30,000 followers!
In addition to parenting and cooking, Tyler also participates in regional cooking competitions. He is a former pitmaster who still loves steaks and smoked brisket.
Education
Old Dominion University, bachelor's in exercise science
