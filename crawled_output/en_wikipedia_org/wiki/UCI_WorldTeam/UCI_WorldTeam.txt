From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Men's professional cycling team of the highest UCI ranking (2015–)
A UCI WorldTeam (2015–present), previously UCI ProTeam (2005–2014), is the Union Cycliste Internationale's (UCI) men's cycling team of the highest category in professional road cycling — the UCI World Tour or UCI ProTour, respectively.
