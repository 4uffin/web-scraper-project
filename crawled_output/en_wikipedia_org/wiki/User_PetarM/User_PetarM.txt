Hi everyone. My name is Petar Milošević. Beside English Wiki i have accounts on Serbian Wikipedia, Slovenian Wikipedia, Russian Wikipedia and Wikimedia Commons where I contribute most. You can contact me on e-mail petarmslo@gmail.com (available at request)
My photography
I am using Micro Four Thirds system: Olympus OM-D E-M1, Panasonic Lumix DMC-G80 and drone DJI 3 Mavic for aerial photo. Sometimes I use film cameras as well.
