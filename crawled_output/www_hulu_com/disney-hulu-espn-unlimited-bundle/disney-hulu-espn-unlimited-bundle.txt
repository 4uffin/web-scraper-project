Existing ESPN+ subscribers have been converted into ESPN Select subscribers, but they will continue to have access to the same ESPN+ service. ESPN+ is available to fans in both our ESPN Select and Unlimited Plans. ESPN Select includes ESPN+ content only. Fans who want ESPN+ exclusively may subscribe to the ESPN Select plan.
