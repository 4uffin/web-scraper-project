Have questions about your gift?
When does my gift expire?
Your recipient has one year from the purchase date to redeem their gift.
Can I purchase multiple gift plans?
Yes! It’s easy to give Pluralsight to every learner in your life—just repeat the checkout process for each gift.
Can I gift a business plan?
Business plans aren’t available to gift at this time. Instead, consider gifting a Complete or Core Tech plan.
Can my gift be refunded?
Unfortunately, refunds will not be offered for gifted plans.
