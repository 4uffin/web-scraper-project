1 of 10Who's Who in 'Demon Slayer: Infinity Castle'?1 of 10Tanjiro KamadoTanjiro was a humble coal burner when Muzan Kibutsuji, the King of the Demons, slaughtered his family and infected his little sister with demon blood. Driven to save his sister from her fate, Tanjiro became a member of the Demon Slayer Corps, where he mastered legendary swordsman Yoriichi Tsugikuni’s elemental breathing techniques, trained in combat styles, and forged unbreakable bonds with his fellow slayers. Now, Tanjiro and the Demon Slayer Corps have uncovered the location of the Infinity Castle, where Muzan and his most powerful demons await. With everything on the line, Tanjiro must face his deadliest challenge yet that will push his strength, training, and courage to the limit.Voiced by Natsuki Hanae (Japanese cast) and Zack Aguilar (English cast).
