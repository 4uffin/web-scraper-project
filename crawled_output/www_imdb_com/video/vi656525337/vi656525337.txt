At a remote Icelandic retreat, "A Murder at the End of the World" follows Gen Z sleuth Darby Hart (Emma Corrin) as she races to solve a deadly puzzle while trapped with a mysterious billionaire and his guests. The beloved trio of "Only Murders in the Building" continues their podcast-fueled investigations in their Manhattan apartment building, while the dark comedy thriller "Bodkin" takes viewers to the Irish coast where amateur detectives unravel the truth behind troubling disappearances. Natasha Lyonne shines in "Poker Face" as Charlie, whose uncanny ability to detect lies leads her on a cross-country journey solving crimes, while "Irish Blood" adds its own distinctive flavor to this collection of shows that prove the most compelling mysteries aren't always solved by traditional law enforcement.
