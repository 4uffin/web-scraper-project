As generative AI becomes more ubiquitous, a paradox has emerged: The more deeply we integrate the technology into our workflows, the more indispensable human skills become. These “soft” skills—like problem framing, collaboration, and creativity—encompass the uniquely human abilities and behaviors that will enable people to make the most working alongside gen AI.SGSagar Goel
