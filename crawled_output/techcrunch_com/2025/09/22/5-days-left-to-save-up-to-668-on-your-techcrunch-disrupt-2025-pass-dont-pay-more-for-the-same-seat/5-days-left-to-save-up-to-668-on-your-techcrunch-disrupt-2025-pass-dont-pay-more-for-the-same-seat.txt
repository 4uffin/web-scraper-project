5 days left to save up to $668 on your TechCrunch Disrupt 2025 pass
TechCrunch Events
7:00 AM PDT · September 22, 2025
There’s still time — but just barely. Register for TechCrunch Disrupt 2025 by September 26 and save up to $668 before prices rise.
Disrupt 2025 gives you an exclusive look at tomorrow’s tech. Founders can connect with the right investors and partners to scale their startups. Investors can discover their next portfolio company. Tech innovators and visionaries can glimpse the future of technology while building the connections that help them grow.
With 10,000 tech and VC leaders in one place, you’re bound to find the insights and relationships you need. Join us for the 20th anniversary of TechCrunch — Disrupt 2025 takes place October 27-29 at San Francisco’s Moscone West. Register now to pocket big savings before this week ends.
Image Credits:TechCrunch
At Disrupt 2025, you’ll:
Hear from leaders in the tech ecosystem like Aaron Levie (Box), Raquel Urtasun (Waabi), Max Altschuler (GTMfund), Mati Staniszewski (ElevenLabs), Anton Osika (Lovable), Tekedra Mawakana (Waymo), Elad Gil, and 250+ more.
Image Credits:Steve Jennings / Getty Images
Connect with thousands of founders, investors, and operators. Discover your next startup investment, find the right investor to scale, or meet the key operators shaping the tech landscape. From curated meetings to impromptu encounters in the Expo Hall, or a spontaneous introduction at the investor/founder-exclusive Deal Flow Cafe — there’s no better place to forge the connections that help you grow.
Image Credits:TechCrunch / TechCrunch
Explore what’s next across all five industry stages over three full days. Eyeballing an IPO? Head to the Going Public Stage. Thinking of launching, or already launched? The Builders Stage offers three days of programming packed with tactical insights to help founders at every stage scale faster. Scaling an AI startup, or just fascinated by AI? Don’t miss two full days at the AI Stage. Curious about space tech? The Space Stage delivers a full day of orbital insights. And on the Disrupt Stage, hear from some of the biggest names in today’s tech.
Image Credits:Slava Blazer Photography
Experience the intense Startup Battlefield 200 pitch competition, where the top 20 TechCrunch-vetted early-stage startups pitch on the Disrupt Stage for a $100,000 prize. Hear seasoned VCs give feedback on what makes a winning pitch, and what it takes to build a viable startup.
Image Credits:Kimberly White / Getty Images
Get hands-on in roundtables and ask your burning questions to top tech voices in breakout sessions, gaining startup-ready and tech insights. Walk away with new connections and actionable takeaways to fuel real growth.
Image Credits:TechCrunch
Witness the future of tech with 100+ startups showcasing in the bustling Expo Hall and throughout the venue. Meet brands pushing innovation to the forefront of the tech ecosystem, and try hands-on demos of their cutting-edge products. Want your brand in the spotlight for all three days?
Image Credits:Eric Slomonson, The Photo Group
September 26 is the last day for ticket savings
You have until September 26 at 11:59 p.m. PT to secure up to $668 in ticket savings. Whether you’re a founder looking to scale or prepare for an IPO, a tech innovator seeking a firsthand look at tomorrow’s tech, or an investor hunting for the next big opportunity, don’t miss Disrupt 2025. Register your pass now.
