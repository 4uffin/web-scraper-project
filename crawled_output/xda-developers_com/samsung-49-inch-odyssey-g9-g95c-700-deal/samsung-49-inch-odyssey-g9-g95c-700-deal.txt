If you've been thinking about expanding your screen real estate to epic proportions, then Samsung's 49-inch ultrawide gaming monitor is going to be for you. Not only are you getting an impressive size, but there's also fantastic performance, with the monitor featuring a 240Hz refresh rate and 1ms response time. The best part about this monitor is that it's now down to its lowest price ever, falling to just $700 for a very limited time.
What's great about the Samsung Odyssey G9 G95C gaming monitor?
This is one of those monitors that becomes a no-brainer once it reaches a certain price point. It features a 49-inch panel with a 1000R curve that delivers an immersive experience that's perfect for gaming and enjoying your media. As mentioned before, you also get excellent performance numbers as well, with a 240Hz refresh rate and 1ms response time.
The monitor also takes things a step further with support for AMD's FreeSync Premium Pro technology. In addition, you also get great connectivity as well with two HDMI, DisplayPort, and two USB-A ports. This means you'll be able to connect other important devices to this monitor to make it your central entertainment hub. There's also customizable RGB lighting as well. It's an impressive combination for a great price, especially if you've been looking to go with something larger than what you're used to. The Samsung Odyssey G9 G95C gaming monitor delivers everything you could want for a very fair price thanks to this excellent discount.
