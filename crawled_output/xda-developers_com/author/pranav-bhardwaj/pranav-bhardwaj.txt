About Pranav Bhardwaj
Pranav Bhardwaj is a seasoned Tech Writer with a passion for simplifying complex tech issues. Pranav specializes in Windows OS troubleshooting, app reviews, and iOS and Android troubleshooting, offering readers clear, actionable solutions to their tech challenges. Writing since 2019, he has contributed to leading publications like MakeUseOf, SlashGear, MSPowerUser, OnMSFT, TutorialsPoint, MalwareFox, and WindowsChimp.
