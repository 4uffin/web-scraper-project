This is the laptop to get if you're trying to buy something for gaming on a budget. Not only does it come from a reputable brand, but the specifications are also pretty good, considering the $580 price tag. This laptop has more than enough power for your favorite AAA games and can even work with your favorite photo or video editing software.
Worth taking a look
The Lenovo LOQ 15 features an AMD Ryzen 5 7235HS processor, 12GB of RAM, and an Nvidia GeForce RTX 4050 GPU. What's great is that you can upgrade the RAM if you want, so if you're looking for more power down the line, that's definitely a possibility. It has a large 15.6-inch 16:9 screen with an impressive refresh rate that can reach up to 144Hz. In order to provide the best visuals possible, the laptop supports Nvidia's G-Sync, and also has excellent cooling, with a large venting system in the rear. The laptop also has plenty of ports, like USB-C, three USB-A, HDMI, Ethernet, 3.5mm audio, Wi-Fi 6, and Bluetooth 5.1. There's also a 720p webcam if you need to hop on any video calls.
You're getting more than enough to play games here, and this is a laptop that can be easily used for work as well. Of course, it is on the larger side, so it's not the most portable, but it does pack some impressive power for the price. Just be quick about picking it up Best Buy won't have this deal for long.
