It estimates your grade based on the information you provide, but it should not be taken as your actual grade.Can AI Grader check for plagiarism?No, AI Grader doesn’t check for plagiarism. Use our Plagiarism Checker agent and Citation Finder agent to make sure your work is original and thoroughly cited.Will my school or professor know I used AI Grader?AI Grader is designed for students to review and refine their assignments before submission.
