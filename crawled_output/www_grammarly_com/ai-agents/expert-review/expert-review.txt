Click the icon for the Expert Review agent on the right side of your screen to see AI suggestions inspired by how subject-matter experts might approach your topic. Review the suggestions and choose which ones you’d like to apply. Want more feedback? You can add additional expert perspectives to get another round of AI-powered suggestions or move forward with your edits based on the initial feedback. Learn more about Expert Review here.Is it free to use Expert Review?Yes! Free Grammarly users can access Expert Review in docs. You can view up to five suggestions each day for free. To gain unlimited access, upgrade to Grammarly Pro.How are the experts chosen?Experts are chosen based on the context and goals of your writing. Expert Review analyzes your work and draws from relevant subject-matter expertise to provide you with targeted feedback. As your writing evolves, the agent adjusts which experts it references, ensuring guidance stays relevant. You also have the option of adding and selecting your own preferred experts for even more personalized support.Can I choose which experts I want to see feedback from?Yes, once you've clicked the icon for the Expert Review agent, the Choose Experts button will appear in the top right. The agent will suggest recommended experts based on your document. You can also specify the topic areas that you want to source specialized feedback from or type the specific names of experts you want included.Is using Expert Review cheating?Expert Review is designed to help you revise and strengthen your writing, not to do the work for you. The suggestions it provides are generated by AI and inspired by how experts might approach your topic, based on what you’ve already written. The agent offers ideas, but it’s up to you to decide whether they fit your tone, structure, and key points. Every instructor has different expectations when it comes to using AI, so it’s always a good idea to check with them first.What are AI agents and what do they do?AI agents are smart digital helpers that take action for you by, for example, providing expert feedback, predicting grades and reader reactions, or finding sources. Unlike the typical tools you’re used to, AI agents use your context to offer more useful writing support. You can try Grammarly’s agents anytime at app.grammarly.com, with more ways to access them coming soon!Where can I use agents?Sign in to your Grammarly account (make sure to sign up if you’re not already using Grammarly). Open a new doc in app.grammarly.com to start using Grammarly’s AI agents. On the right-hand side panel, you’ll see a series of agents ready and waiting to support your writing. And soon you’ll be able to use Grammarly agents on the 500,000+ websites and apps where you currently use Grammarly.How do AI agents use my data?When you ask agents for help, they rely on the context of your document to make useful suggestions, and the data in your working environment is only temporarily stored while they do so.How can I find more agents?To find more Grammarly AI agents, make sure you’re signed in to your Grammarly account and visit our updated writing experience at app.grammarly.com. Open a new doc and click on the bar of icons on the right-hand side panel of your screen. You’ll see a list of available agents that will help you write more effectively from start to finish.
