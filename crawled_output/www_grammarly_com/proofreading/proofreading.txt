For academic writing, proofreading helps eliminate errors that could harm your grade. Professionals need proofreading to ensure their writing reflects their authority and credibility.Why should I use an AI proofreader?AI proofreaders help you quickly correct sentence-level mistakes. Many go beyond grammar, spelling, and punctuation to offer suggestions to improve the clarity of your writing.
