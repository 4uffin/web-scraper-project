In addition to the online cash, you'll also earn an additional 3% cash back in the category of your choice for the first year, for a total of up to 6% cash back in that category.These offers are similar, both offer a bonus worth $200 with slightly different spending requirements. While the Blue Cash Everyday card gives you roughly twice as long to meet the spending goal compared to the Customized Cash card, the amount you need to spend is twice as high, balancing it out.The largest difference comes with the Customized Cash Rewards card, offering an additional 3% cash back in the category of your choosing for the first year.Winner: Bank of America Customized Cash Rewards. It earns additional rewards in a category of your choice the first year.RewardsThe Amex Blue Cash Everyday card is a great option for people looking to earn cash back on their grocery and gas spending. You can change your category up to once per month.If you find yourself spending a lot at grocery stores, then the Amex Blue Cash Everyday is likely the better pick.
