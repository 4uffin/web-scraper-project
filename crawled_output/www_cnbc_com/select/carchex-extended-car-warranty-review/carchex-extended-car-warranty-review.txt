But it doesn’t cover luxury vehicles or RVs and, unlike some competitors, there’s no discount on collision repairs or option to cover routine maintenance.CarChex FAQsWhat is an extended car warranty?An extended car warranty covers repairs to specified parts after the manufacturer's warranty expires. You’re protected for a set number of years or miles, and your plan can cover nearly the entire car (bumper-to-bumper) or just named components, such as the powertrain or electrical system. An extended warranty may be provided by the car manufacturer and sold by the dealership, or it may come from a third-party company like CarChex.How much does a CarChex extended warranty coverage cost?For a five-year Platinum plan for a 2021 Ford Escape with 50,000 miles, we were quoted a rate of $191 per month for 24 months.Will CarChex provide a rental car while my vehicle is being repaired?Yes, all CarChex plans include reimbursement for a rental car if you have a covered breakdown repair.
