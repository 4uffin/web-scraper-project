New Speakers Announced: 5th Palm Beach CorpGov Forum | Preview Panelists & Topics

CorpGov, IPO Edge and PE Edge will host the fifth annual Palm Beach CorpGov Forum on Wednesday, Nov. 5 and Thursday, Nov, 6, 2025, at the Palm Beach Lake Pavilion, located waterfront with sweeping views of Palm Beach Island. The Forum will feature panels and firesides from 1pm to 5pm. Networking receptions will follow each day after the event from 5pm to 7:30pm.​ REGISTER HERE Watch the 2024 CorpGov Forum Highlights:
Our audience will include approximately 300 attendees including institutional investors, board directors, family offices/HNWIs, attorneys, investment bankers and key advisors.​ The event will be digitized into a report published on CorpGov and by its content partners (under relevant company stock tickers) Yahoo Finance, Bloomberg Terminals, Reuters via LSEG Workspace, and AlphaSense, and filmed with multiple 4K video cameras and professional editing.
NOTE FOR SERVICE PROVIDERS: Early Bird special of $500 is available until Oct. 1. For sponsorship, speaker or media enquiries, email Editor@CorpGov.com   Preliminary topics include:​
Private Equity and Venture Capital
Directors on Private Boards
IPO Execution ​​
Shareholder Activism​
Contested M&A
Industry Panels: Cybersecurity, Real Estate, Blockchain, AI, Sports and more
