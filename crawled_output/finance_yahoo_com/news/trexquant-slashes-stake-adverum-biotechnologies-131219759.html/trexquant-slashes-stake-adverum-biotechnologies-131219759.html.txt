Rameen Kasana
Sun, Sep 21, 2025, 9:12 AM 1 min read
In this article:
ADVM
Adverum Biotechnologies, Inc. (NASDAQ:ADVM) is among the best fundamentally strong penny stocks to buy right now. During the first quarter, Trexquant Investment LP reduced its stake in Adverum Biotechnologies, Inc. (NASDAQ:ADVM) by 80.5%. Following the offload of 77,303 shares, the institutional investor now owns 18,694 shares of the company’s stock, valued at approximately $82,000. According to the recent 13F filing with the SEC, the firm has an ownership of 0.09%.
Last month, Adverum Biotechnologies, Inc. (NASDAQ:ADVM) announced its financial results, highlighting its pipelines and future plans. The management revealed impressive advancement in the ARTEMIS Phase 3 trial, with enrollment fueled by growing enthusiasm from retina specialists and patients. The company seems to be well ahead of schedule, and as momentum grows, strategic partnership talks continue to build.
