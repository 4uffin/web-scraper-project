and Josh Lipton

In this video:
CBRL

BLSH
Asking for a Trend host Josh Lipton previews the biggest stories to come for Wednesday, September 17, namely the Federal Reserve's latest interest rate decision, corporate earnings from companies like General Mills (GIS), Bullish (BLSH), and Cracker Barrel Old Country Store (CBRL), and the latest data on US housing starts and building permits. To watch more expert insights and analysis on the latest market action, check out more Asking for a Trend.
