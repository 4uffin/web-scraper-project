Trump, China's Xi to discuss TikTok deal. What is the app worth?
Yahoo Finance Video
and Julie Hyman
Wed, Sep 17, 2025, 9:37 AM
In this video:
ORCL
In their latest round of trade negotiations, US and Chinese officials reached an agreement this week on a preliminary framework for the sale of the popular social media app TikTok. President Trump and China's President Xi Jinping will further discuss the deal on a call. Cloud service provider Oracle (ORCL) — which hosts TikTok — is a frontrunner to acquire the app. Morning Brief host Julie Hyman reports the latest news and breaks down TikTok's estimated valuation in the US. To watch more expert insights and analysis on the latest market action, check out more Morning Brief.
