Metaplanet Inc. engages in hotel management operations in Japan. It operates through Hotel Business and Other segments. The company is also involved in various fields of Web3, blockchain, and NFT-related businesses; and real estate and investment services. In addition, it provides specialized consulting services to assist businesses in adopting Bitcoin and Bitcoin-related media, events, and platform
