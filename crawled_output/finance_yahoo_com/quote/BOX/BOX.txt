Box, Inc. Overview Software - Infrastructure / Technology
Box, Inc. provides a cloud content management platform that enables organizations of various sizes to manage and share their content from anywhere on any device in the United States and Japan. The company's Software-as-a-Service platform enables users to work with their content as they need from secure external collaboration, workspaces to e-signature processes, and content workflows improving employee productivity and accelerating business processes. It also offers web, mobile, and desktop applications of its solutions on a platform, as well as the ability to develop custom applications. The company was formerly known as Box.net, Inc. and changed its name to Box, Inc. in November 2011. Box, Inc. was incorporated in 2005 and is headquartered in Redwood City, California. www.box.com
