Better Home & Finance Holding Company Overview Mortgage Finance / Financial Services
Better Home & Finance Holding Company operates as a homeownership company in the United States. The company provides government-sponsored enterprise GSE conforming loans, U.S. Federal Housing Administration FHA insured loans, U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs VA guaranteed loans, and jumbo loans to GSEs, banks, insurance companies, asset managers, and mortgage real estate investment trusts. It also offers real estate agent services, title insurance and settlement services, and homeowners insurance services. The company formerly known as Better Mortgage Corporation and changed its name to Better Home & Finance Holding Company in August 2023. Better Home & Finance Holding Company is headquartered in New York, New York. better.com
