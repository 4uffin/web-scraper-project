Camden Property Trust Overview REIT - Residential / Real Estate
Camden Property Trust, an S&P 500 Company, is a real estate company primarily engaged in the ownership, management, development, redevelopment, acquisition, and construction of multifamily apartment communities. Camden owns and operates 173 properties containing 59,046 apartment homes across the United States. Upon completion of 4 properties currently under development, the Company's portfolio will increase to 60,577 apartment homes in 177 properties. Camden has been recognized as one of the 100 Best Companies to Work for by FORTUNE magazine for 18 consecutive years, most recently ranking 18. www.camdenliving.com
