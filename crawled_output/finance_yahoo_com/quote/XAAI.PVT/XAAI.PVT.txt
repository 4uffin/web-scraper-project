xAI Overview
xAI is an artificial intelligence company focused on developing advanced AI technologies with the goal of understanding and aligning AI systems with human values. The company aims to create AI that addresses concerns related to advanced AI models. xAI's core product offering is Grok, an AI language model which is offered to premium users on the X platform. The company was founded by Elon Musk and is part of Musk's broader vision for AI development.
