Trump Media & Technology Group Corp. Overview Internet Content & Information / Communication Services
Trump Media & Technology Group Corp. engages in social media and streaming services in the United States. The company operates Truth Social, a social media platform for free expression; and Truth+, a streaming platform focusing on news, Christian content, and family friendly programming. It also offers Truth.Fi, a financial service and FinTech brand that incorporates America First investment vehicles. Trump Media & Technology Group Corp. was founded in 2021 and is headquartered in Sarasota, Florida. tmtgcorp.com
