This cozy hotel has managed to keep the character and charm of when it was first constructed while also updated the amenities to include all of the modern-day conveniences that that travelers love. Whether guests are looking to explore Mission Valley, San Diego, or both, The Atwood is a superb choice to spend your stay.
The Atwood has five different floor plans available for guests to choose from. Worth noting are the two floorplans that are ADA accessible, one with a king sized bed and the other with two queens. Travelers with pets will be happy to know that The Atwood has designated a room just for them, a pet-friendly room with two beds. All of the rooms come with complimentary wifi, in-room coffee maker, flat screen TV, microwave, refrigerator, alarm clock, and ironing board.
Should guests want something to eat while they are staying at The Atwood, all they have to do is venture down to the first floor to try out the built-in restaurant, Crust Kitchen. Crust Kitchen is a beautiful eatery with high vaulted ceilings, modern light fixtures and wraparound windows that let plenty of natural light in. The restaurant is open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The menu here features dishes like strawberry waffles, French toast, breakfast sandwiches, omelets, pizza, burgers and a traditional Italian spaghetti with meatballs. There is also a standalone smoothie machine, coffee bar and full drink menu for those looking to get something with a little more kick.
