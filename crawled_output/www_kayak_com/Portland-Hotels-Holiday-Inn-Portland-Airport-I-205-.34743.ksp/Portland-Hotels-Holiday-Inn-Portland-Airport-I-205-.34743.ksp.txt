The property is quite large and features 284 rooms for the guests that have a range of amenities. The property is family-friendly, and kids can even stay free when they are using the existing bedding in the rooms. Extra beds and cribs are available. The property is also pet-friendly, but some restrictions will apply.
The rooms at the property have free WiFi, as well as cable channels on the flat-screen TV, a free newspaper, air conditioning, coffee and tea making facilities, daily housekeeping, turndown service, a pillow menu, phone, free local calls, and a private bathroom. It is also possible to request hypoallergenic bedding if needed. Connecting rooms are available, but they are subject to availability.
For an added charge, guests can have a buffet breakfast from 6AM to 10:30 AM each morning. The property also offers an indoor pool and fitness facilities for those who want to unwind and stay in shape, coffee and tea in the common areas, a 24hr front desk, meeting rooms, a business center, and free WiFi in many of the public areas.
In addition to being near the airport, the property is also close to Cascade Station, the Oregon Convention Center, Lloyd Center, Grotto, and more. Nearby dining includes Colwood Grill and Airport Café.
