20 minutes from downtown Cancun. This is a business and family-centered hotel only a couple of miles from the Convention Center. It offers both an All-Inclusive lodging option and a European Plan. This air-conditioned hotel is 20 minutes from Cancun International Airport and has 249 guestrooms available.
All rooms at this hotel are spacious four-star rooms with the security and comfort that guests want. Each room comes with a television, telephone, and complimentary high-speed wireless Internet. There is a hairdryer, dressing table, and a private bathroom with the needed amenities. Room service, laundry service, and wake-up calls are available and rooms come with a safety deposit box.
There are several on-site dining options started with La Fuente Restaurant which is on the ground floor and offers an International buffet spread. Another choice is Albatros Restaurant which is beachside and specializes in lobster dishes and ceviche. There is also a pool bar by the main pool where guests can order burgers, milkshakes, and other snacks. Don Roberto’s Bar & Pizza serves pizza and beverages near the swimming pool while Palenque Lobby Bar serves beer, wine, and cocktails.
When it’s time to have fun, Flamingo Cancun Resort has two swimming pools, a fitness center, Jacuzzi, kids’ club, temazcal, and a massage area. There are also many water and land sports to take part in like volleyball and kayaking.
