Inn & Suites by Wyndham Denver offers guests spacious digs near the Denver Airport. It’s ideal for business travelers, couples, families, and groups looking for well-appointed lodgings in central Denver.
Located in northwestern Denver, Microtel Inn Suites by Wyndham Denver is just a short drive from Denver International Airport. It is also near many points of interest, including the Aurora Mall, Denver Botanic Gardens, Children’s Museum of Denver, Denver Performing Arts Center, and Rocky Mountain Arsenal National Wildlife Refuge.
Accommodations are welcoming and traditional. Guests may choose Standard Rooms with choice of one king, one queen, or two queen beds, as well as accessibility-friendly rooms. All rooms have sitting areas, air temperature controls, flat-screen TVs, and desks. Pets are allowed to stay, and children under 17 stay at the hotel for free.
Each morning, guests are invited to partake in free continental breakfast. The hotel is also close to some of the best dining and drinking hotspots in Denver, including Moonlight Diner, Pho Kitchen 88, Ted’s Montana Grill, Bender’s Brat Haus, and CaraFaye’s Café.
Microtel Inn Suites by Wyndham Denver offers guests free shuttle service to and from the airport around the clock. There is also free parking and 24-hour fitness and business centers to enjoy.
