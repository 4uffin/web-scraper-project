photosOverviewSilver Sevens Hotel and Casino caters to budget travelers who don't mind being a 20-minute walk away from the action on The Strip.
The hotel is located across the street from Paradise Shopping Center, and within a ten-minute walk to plenty of chain restaurants. A five-minute drive takes guests to the renowned Strip, and straight down East Flamingo Road to the famous Bellagio Fountains. Downtown Las Vegas, The Neon Museum, and other popular attractions can take 20-minutes or more depending on traffic. An added convenience to Silver Sevens Hotel is that it’s only five minutes from McCarran International Airport and the hotel offers a free shuttle.
Silver Sevens Hotel offers suite options, but there are also three-room styles available; Premium, Tower, and Patio, all with one king-size bed or two queen-size beds. All rooms come with similar amenities, including 42-inch flat-screen TVs with Pay-Per-View movies, air-conditioning, and free WiFi. Mini-fridges and irons/ironing boards are available on request. Smoking and non-smoking rooms are available, and pet-friendly rooms are also available upon request.
The star attraction at Silver Sevens Hotel is the on-site casino, which offers 800 games plus race and sports book. There's also a small fitness center, a lagoon-style pool with a hot tub, and free parking. Silver Sevens offers three different dining outlets. The 24-hour Sterling Spoon Café is the main restaurant which features a variety of American and Chinese dishes.
