staysOverviewAmbassador Chicago is a boutique hotel with a history. Built in 1926, this hotel was inspired by the jazz area and was host to the city's most elite guests. Offering the best of both worlds, Ambassador Chicago is situated in the quite, affluent section of Chicago while still close enough for guests to be able to explore the city. With a coffeehouse-style cafe and restaurant/bar on the premises, however, guests are able to come back to the hotel to relax and unwind after a day of activities.
Ambassador Chicago has 258 guestrooms with both room and suite options. Spacious rooms feature flat screen televisions, complimentary Wi-Fi, a work area, and bathrooms with bathtubs. Suites are outfitted with a variety of different amenities to suit the needs of any guest. The most luxurious suite is the penthouse suite. Large enough to entertain and decked out in glamorous decor, this suite is an elegant home away from home. Hotel-wide amenities include a fitness center, business center, and valet parking for a fee. Ambassador Chicago is pet friendly so guests are welcome to let their pets tag along on vacation.
Ambassador Chicago offers loyalty programs and discounts to provide the best guest experience for a good price. Guests are also encouraged to keep an eye out for special event packages. Featured now are Valentine's Day getaways to help re-ignite the romance on this special day. Wedding and event packages are also available to make special occasions memorable and business functions enjoyable. With a variety of options and venues to choose from, guests can be sure that their special event will be perfect and exactly how they imagined.
