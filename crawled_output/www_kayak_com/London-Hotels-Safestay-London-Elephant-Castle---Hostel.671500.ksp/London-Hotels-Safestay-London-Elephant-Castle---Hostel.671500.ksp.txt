popular landmarks and destinations. This former headquarters of the Labour Party has been turned into a modern hostel accommodation, just minutes from local food markets, Tower Bridge, the London Eye, and Big Ben. Guests can also visit the Shard, Tate Modern, the Westminster Bridge, Westminster Abbey, and the Houses of Parliament during their stay. The hostel staff can also provide assistance with tour arrangements, attraction tickets and reservations, and even provide sightseeing recommendations during your stay.
The Safestay London Elephant & Castle Hotel has a 24-hour front desk for guest convenience and free WiFi is available throughout the building. There are accessible facilities for disabled guests and lockers for guest belongings. The hostel provides breakfast for a fee, and there is a bar and lounge area in the lobby where a variety of events are hosted. The bar serves snacks and food throughout the day. Daily housekeeping is also provided.
This property offers private rooms that sleep up to three guests, and include their own private bathroom, as well as complimentary coffee and tea, a TV, and complimentary towels and toiletries. Shared rooms are also available, which sleep four to 12 people and include bedside power outlets, bunk privacy curtains, and a shared bathroom. There are a total of 72 rooms at this non-smoking hostel, which does not allow pets.
