The Tennessee State Fairgrounds Sports Arena is six miles away while the Grand Ole Opry is only eight miles from the hotel. This value business hotel is less than two miles from Nashville Metropolitan Airport and offers transportation to and from the airport. The hotel has 155 non-smoking, air-conditioned guest rooms and suites.
Guests can choose from a queen room, king room, or a suite at the hotel. The rooms come with high-speed wireless Internet access and comfortable seating. There is a flat-screen television with cable channels, a refrigerator, and a microwave. The rooms have a private bathroom with a hairdryer, coffeemaker, and iron and ironing board. Suites have a separate bedroom and living area along with a sleeper sofa. A workstation with a desk and chair are also provided.
This hotel offers a fresh, free, hot breakfast in the morning for guests. There are Belgian waffles, sausage, scrambled eggs, oatmeal, fruit, biscuits, gravy, coffee, and more. There is also a 5:30 Kickback with free hot food and cold beverages each day. Popcorn and soda are also available from the lobby each day.
Many restaurants are in the area like Wendy’s, McDonald’s, Sub Depot, Ellendale’s, and Ruby Tuesday.
