It is a smoke-free property that is child-friendly. It is not pet-friendly, but service animals are welcome. Wheelchair accessible accommodations are available.
The hostel offers a range of rooms including male dorms, female dorms, and coed dorms, as well as twin, queen, and economy triple private rooms. In some of the rooms and dorms, there are ensuite bathrooms. Shared hallway bathrooms are available for the others. Some amenities found in the rooms and dorms include free WiFi, desks, blackout drapes, daily housekeeping, and access to local maps and guidebooks.
Each morning a complimentary buffet breakfast is served from 7 AM to 10 AM. Some of the public areas have free WiFi, as well.
