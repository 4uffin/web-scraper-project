Downtown LA, Ace Hotel combines industrial-chic decor with luxury comfort. This hip hotel is set in the landmark 1927 United Artists building.
The spacious rooms are decked out in trendy, upscale decor which aims to capture the LA hipster culture. All rooms feature a king bed, a private wet bar, a flat screen television, custom Revo Radio, free wifi, custom bath products, a stocked minibar, and a luxury bathrobe. Select suites include kitchenettes, an acoustic guitar, and a separate living area.
Guests can browse the onsite shop which sells vintage vinyls, custom clothing, and other boutique goods.
An outdoor pool and onsite fitness center are available for guests to use. A spectacular panoramic view of Downtown LA can be seen from the rooftop bar and lounge, which serves up swanky cocktails as chilled out tunes waft in the background.
There is a coffee bar and a juice bar onsite, as well as drinks available at the rooftop lounge and bar. For dining, guests can enjoy the renowned onsite restaurant, Best Girl, which is known for its creative and vibrant dishes crafted by critically acclaimed chef, Michael Cimarusti. The menu offers specialty dishes, and incorporates creative ingredients, like dandelion, horseradish crema, and honeynut squash. Guests rave about the delectable dishes formed from the unique ingredient combinations.
