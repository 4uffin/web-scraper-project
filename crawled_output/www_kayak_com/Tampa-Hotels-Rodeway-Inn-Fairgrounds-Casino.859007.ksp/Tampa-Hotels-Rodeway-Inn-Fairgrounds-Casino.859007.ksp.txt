Rooms are budget-friendly, clean, and comfortable.
The hotel is situated near the junction of Interstate 4 and Interstate 75, nearby several attractions, restaurants, convenience stores, and shopping centers. Some popular nearby attractions include University of South Florida Tampa, Busch Gardens, Raymond James Stadium, and Seminole Hard Rock Casino.
Comfortable, simple rooms are modernly decorated. All rooms include coffee makers with complimentary coffee, microwaves, mini-fridges, free unlimited high-speed wifi, comfortable bedding, spacious bathrooms, and laundry services.
Other amenities include the large outdoor swimming pool where guests can enjoy lounge chairs on the sundeck, and free parking in the parking area. Business travelers have access to the on-site copy machine, fax machine, free local calls, and business rooms.
Many stunning beaches are within a short driving distance, including the beaches in Clearwater and St. Petersburg, both are less than 45 minutes from the hotel.
There are no kitchens in the rooms, nor is there an on-site restaurant, but there are plenty of spectacular dining options within a stone’s throw of the hotel. Some of the most popular nearby dining options include 40th Street Restaurant, Salem Gyros and Subs, El Paraiso Cafe, Acropolis Greek Taverna, Zambia Steakhouse, and Olive Garden Italian Restaurant.
