staysOverviewLocated on International Drive close to many of the area attractions, the Rosen Inn, Closest to Universal, Orlando offers guests plenty of services and amenities. There are one restaurant and two bars on the property, and guests will find that breakfast is available daily. There is an outdoor pool, a 24-hour fitness center, and a business center for guest convenience. The front desk is manned 24-hours per day, and the staff members are multilingual. They can speak English, French, and Spanish to better help assist guests. The hotel is happy to provide a free shuttle to all of the area theme parks.
There are nine floors in the Rosen Inn, and those floors contain 315 guest rooms. Each room has in-room climate controls, coffee makers, irons, and ironing boards. Plus, the private bathrooms are stocked with free toiletries and hair dryers. The rooms all contain flat-screen TVs, and guests can watch cable TV channels or pay-per-view movies. WiFi is included in the room rate, and guests can make local calls without incurring any additional charges. The rooms are outfitted with safes, microwaves, and refrigerators. Rooms can be booked with either one king-sized bed or two double beds.
Guests can easily walk to the Universal Orlando Resort which is just 2 minutes away on foot. Also nearby is ICON Orlando, Universal CityWalk, Universal Islands of Adventure, and Universal Studios Florida. Shopping options nearby include the Orlando International Premium Outlets.
