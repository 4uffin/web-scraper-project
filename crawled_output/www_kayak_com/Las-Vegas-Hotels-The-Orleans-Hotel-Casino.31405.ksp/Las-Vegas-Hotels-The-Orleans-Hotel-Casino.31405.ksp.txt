It offers guests the best of both worlds: the lights, gambling, and location of Las Vegas alongside the attitude, cuisine, and aesthetic of New Orleans. Guest rooms are fitted with air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, flat screen TVs, and coffee-making facilities.
Guest can grab a bite with the in-room service or visit any of the15 restaurants and 6 bars/lounges located onsite. Sbarro, Subway and Fuddruckers provide convenient options for a casual meal, while the The Prime Rib Loft and Alder & Birch offer a more upscale experience.
The casino offers a variety of games, including over 2,600 video poker, slots, and video keno machines. It also features table games like craps, blackjack, baccarat, Pai Gow poker, and more. Sports fans can also use the Race and Sports Book to place bets on any of the major sporting events. Recreational amenities at the hotel include a large spa tub, two outdoor pools, and a fully-equipped fitness center. In addition, the resort boasts a theater and a 70-lane bowling center.
The hotel provides convenient access to many local aattractions. It’s less than four miles from the Fashion Show mall and the Thomas and Mack center. The McCarran International Airport is roughly 10 minutes away. The Orleans Hotel & Casino is also situated within a 15-minute drive of the Las Vegas Convention Center.
