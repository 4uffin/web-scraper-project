This resort destination is located on the beach in the main tourist area of Cancun, just 15 minutes from Cancun International Airport. Shopping, tourist attractions, and plenty of activities are nearby this hotel. The resort offers beautiful views from all 400 of the guest rooms, suites, and villas that have been updated with modern amenities and high-tech creature comforts.
The Emporio Hotel & Suites Cancun resort offers plenty of activities and equipment for outdoor use during your stay, including snorkeling equipment, volleyball, golfing and golf clubs, board games, bike tours, water aerobics, dance classes, and more. The hotel also offers Club Piruetas, a kids’ club, which offers childcare along with activities and games for kids’ entertainment. There is a large pool area with a kids’ area, a playground, and tennis courts on the property of this resort, which also features a pool bar and two restaurants. The resort offers an on site convenience store, gym, business center, laundry service, and concierge services. Free on site parking is provided for all guests.
Rooms offer the choice of resort views or ocean views, with select oceanfront suites that include private balconies. All guest rooms include complimentary WiFi, luxury bedding, flat-screen TVs with premium channels, modern private baths with deluxe amenities, and more. Upgraded suites offer additional living space, premium amenities, and better views.
