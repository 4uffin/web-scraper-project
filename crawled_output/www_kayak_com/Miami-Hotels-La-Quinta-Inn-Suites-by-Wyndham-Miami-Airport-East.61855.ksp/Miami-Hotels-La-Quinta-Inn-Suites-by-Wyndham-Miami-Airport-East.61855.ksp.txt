staysOverviewLocated just a mile from the Miami International Airport, La Quinta Inn & Suites Miami Airport East is a convenient stop for busy travelers. Whether guests are planning a short layover for one night or several days as a fun vacation, the hotel has plenty of amenities and nearby attractions to make the stay more comfortable.
Convenience is a prime feature at La Quinta Inn & Suites. Not only is it close to the airport, but the hotel also provides a free shuttle from the airport, so tired travelers can get to a soft bed with as little hassle as possible. The free Bright Side Breakfast makes it easy to fill up before heading out for the day. This tasty menu features waffles, cereal, muffins, and unlimited coffee or juice.
Additional amenities include an outdoor swimming pool, where guests can relax and catch some sun. Inside the rooms, guests will find flat-panel televisions with 30 HD channels, a hair dryer, coffee maker, ironing equipment, and plug-and-play abilities for electronic devices. These room amenities ensure that guests can enjoy the conveniences and comforts of home, even when they are away.
La Quinta Inn & Suites’ shuttle service does more than simply take guests too and from the nearby airport. It also delivers guests to the Port of Miami, which is helpful for tourists who plan to board a cruise. The hotel also allows guests to leave a vehicle in the parking lot for up to 7 days, meaning they can avoid expensive parking fees at other locations.

Guests can stop by Coconut Grove, Parrot Jungle, and the Mall of America. Seasonal attractions include the Orange Bowl for sports fans.
