It also provides free Wi-Fi, a free shuttle service and a coffee bar.
Sapporo Excel Hotel Tokyu has recently undergone refurbishment and offers a 24-hour reception, bicycle rental and a car rental desk. It also features laundry facilities, limited hours room service and luggage storage.
The rooms are air conditioned and include movies-on-demand and a bathtub. After settling in to their room, guests can explore the area with use of Nakajima-Koen Station, which is a short walk from the hotel.
On-site dining options include Karamatsu and L’Arbre Restaurant, which both serve dinner. In the evening, guests can enjoy a drink in the cozy lounge bar. There is also the added convenience of an in-house restaurant.
New Chitose Airport is around a 60-minute car ride from Excel Hotel Tokyu Sapporo and Susukino Station is within walking distance.
