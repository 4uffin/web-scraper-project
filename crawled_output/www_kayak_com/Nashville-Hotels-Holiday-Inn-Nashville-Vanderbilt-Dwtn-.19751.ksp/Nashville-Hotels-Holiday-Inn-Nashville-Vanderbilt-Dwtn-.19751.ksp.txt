In the heart of the city, the Holiday Inn Nashville-Vanderbilt (Dwtn) is minutes from the city’s most popular attractions. The hotel is across the street from Centennial Park, which features a full-size Parthenon replica in the middle of a lush, green park. Broadway, downtown Nashville attractions, and Vanderbilt Children’s Hospital are all a quick drive from the hotel. In fact, Holiday Inn offers a free shuttle service to these and other destinations within two and one-half miles of the hotel.
The Holiday Inn Nashville-Vanderbilt showcases recently renovated rooms with new beds, furniture, and carpet. Guestrooms feature modern amenities including microwaves, refrigerators, iron/ironing boards, black-out shades, coffeemakers, complimentary morning newspapers, and cribs upon request. Each guestroom has a balcony facing either Centennial Park or Vanderbilt Stadium.
Holiday Inn Nashville-Vanderbilt’s on-site dining option, the Commodore Grille, is open seven days a week and serves a full breakfast buffet each morning. Along with live music, the restaurant also serves appetizers and dinner each night. For families, up to four kids ages 11 and under eat free at the Holiday Inn, any time of the day.
Other convenient hotel amenities include a 24-hour business center with printer and copier, an outdoor pool, a fitness center, free parking, and a cocktail lounge.
The Holiday Inn Nashville-Vanderbilt is located in a prime downtown location near Vanderbilt, downtown Nashville, and a multitude of shops and restaurants.
