The grounds are beautiful and perfect for snapping memorable social media photographs. In addition to many onsite conveniences, London Kensington Holland Park Hostel is near the Holland Park and High Street tube stations. Getting around will be a breeze. The closest airports to the hostel are London City Airport, London Heathrow Airport, and London Gatwick Airport.
When staying at London Kensington Holland Park Hostel, guests can choose between private rooms and shared rooms. Both options are great for those on a budget. The private rooms sleep up to four people, while the shared rooms sleep between 4-21. Private rooms come with an en-suite bathroom, free towels, toiletries, and free tea and coffee. The rooms are decorated in bold colors and modern furniture. Shared rooms are wonderful for those traveling for school trips, hen parties, bachelor parties, and more. Shared areas have everything from personal reading lights and storage areas to secure key card access and power sockets.
Guests of the London Kensington Holland Park Hostel can enjoy breakfast at the venue for a small fee. Breakfast is served from 7AM to 10 AM so that visitors can fuel up for their day of fun.
From the hostel, guests can easily visit famous spots like Notting Hill, Hyde Park, and Kensington Palace. All are less than a mile away. With the help of public transportation, other must-see sights include Buckingham Palace, London Eye, Big Ben, Piccadilly Circus, and Portobello Road Market.
