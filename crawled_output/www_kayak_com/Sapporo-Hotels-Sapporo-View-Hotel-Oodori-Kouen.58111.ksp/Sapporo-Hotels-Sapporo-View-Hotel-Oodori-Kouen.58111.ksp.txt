most popular destination cities. Its rooms, location, and amenities make this hotel a wonderful choice for solo travelers, couples, families, and groups, particularly those interested in a central location.
Set in the heart of Sapporo in Hokkaido Prefecture, Sapporo View Hotel Oodori Kouen is about 25 miles from New Chitose Airport and close to many of the area’s top attractions. While staying here, guests can enjoy proximity to such sites as Sapporo Clock Tower, Hokkaido University Botanical Gardens, Sapporo Mitsukoshi, and Sapporo Dome.
Accommodations come in either Western or Japanese style furnishings. Guests may choose Single Rooms with one single bed, Premium Twin Rooms with two single beds, Rooms with tatami areas, two single beds, and two futon beds, Japanese-style Quadruple Rooms with four futon beds, or Suites with saunas and two single beds. All rooms are equipped free Wi-Fi, private bathrooms, flat-screen TVs, workstation, and refrigerators.
Each morning, guests are invited to partake in a tasty breakfast service that includes continental and buffet-style meals. The hotel’s two onsite restaurants, Ruhua and Audrey Grill Buffet and Restaurant Bar, craft delectable Chinese and Japanese grilled fare respectively.
In terms of services and amenities, Sapporo View Hotel Oodori Kouen has plenty to delight its guests. Guests can enjoy massage services or rent bikes or cars to better explore the city, while the 24-hour front desk can provide currency exchange, luggage storage, and concierge services.
