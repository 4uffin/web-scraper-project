We Found Pyrex and Rubbermaid Food Storage Containers Starting at $5, Including Meal Prep Options

Never underestimate the importance of reliable food storage containers. They keep leftovers fresh, prevent your lunch from spilling in your bag, and help marinate foods. If yours are looking worse for wear, grab these 10 Pyrex and Rubbermaid containers and sets—all under $30 at Target.
The retailer is home to both beloved brands at affordable prices, like this full set of containers for just $5. Plus, there are options for every need, including meal prep sets and leftover containers, and you can choose between glass options, ones with locking lids, and microwave- and dishwasher-safe finds.
Whether you love second day spaghetti or don’t want to find salad dressing drenching your work bag, keep scrolling for Pyrex and Rubbermaid containers worth grabbing.

This set of five containers with lids will keep your ingredients fresh. Each one holds up to three cups of food and is made with the brand’s signature tempered glass that can safely be placed in the microwave or freezer. The accompanying lids are tight-fitting for a secure seal, and once the week is done, everything can be placed in the dishwasher for easy cleaning.
Rubbermaid 4-Pack TakeAlongs Food Storage Containers
Target
$5 at Target
If you love leftovers, these containers make them a breeze to store. They fit just over 5 cups of food and stack in the refrigerator without taking up too much space. The lids seal with a click so you know when they’re secure, and there are graduation marks on the sides of the bowls so you can determine portion sizes. Though they’re plastic, they are BPA-free and freezer-safe, and the bases can be placed in the microwave.
Rubbermaid 10-Piece Brilliance Leakproof Food Storage Containers
Target
$27 at Target
Stock up on a variety of sizes thanks to this all-in-one set. It comes with two 1.3-cup and 3.2-cup containers as well as a large capacity 9.6-cup option. They’re made from BPA-free Tritan plastic that won’t absorb stains or odors, and they stack for easy storage. The lids are also 100 percent leakproof and designed to carry items like soups, stews, and sauces that are prone to spilling. Plus, they’re dishwasher-, microwave-, and freezer-safe.
Pyrex Simply Store Tinted 6-Piece Lidded Round Storage Set
Target
$20 at Target
This set of Pyrex storage is ideal if you’re looking for versatility. It comes with three sizes—2-cup, 4-cup, and 7-cup—that can be used for holding prepped veggies and fruit, keeping leftovers fresh, and even marinating meat. The glass is tempered, so it won’t stain or absorb odors, and it can be placed in the microwave or a pre-heated oven without shattering.
