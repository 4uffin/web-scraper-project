John Mitzewich
John Mitzewich—aka Chef John—has produced more than 1,500 cooking videos for Allrecipes.  He's the actor, director, and screenwriter whose hands and voice confidently walk you through cooking, baking, and general kitchen techniques. Chef John has been teaching viewers how to cook on his YouTube channel Food Wishes since 2007, earning 953M views and 4.25M subscribers.Go to our complete collection of recipes and videos: Food Wishes with Chef JohnChef John's Appetizers and SnacksChef John's Breakfast and BrunchChef John's DessertsChef John's Main DishesChef John's SaladsChef John's Side DishesChef John's Soups and Stews
About Allrecipes
Home cooks are our heroes; it's as simple as that. Allrecipes, a People Inc. Brand, is the world’s largest community-driven food site, providing trusted resources to more than 60 million home cooks each month. Every day, cooks from around the world publish doably delicious recipes, while our team of editorial and kitchen professionals cover the world's top food news and trends.
