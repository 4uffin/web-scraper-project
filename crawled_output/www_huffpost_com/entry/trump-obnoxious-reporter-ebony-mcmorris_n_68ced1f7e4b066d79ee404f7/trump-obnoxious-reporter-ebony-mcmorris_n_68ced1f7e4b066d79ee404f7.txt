Ebony McMorris replied after President Donald Trump insulted her at the White House.By Hilary HansonDeputy Editor, Trends, HuffPostSep 20, 2025, 01:19 PM EDTLEAVE A COMMENTLOADINGERROR LOADINGPresident Donald Trump lashed out at a journalist during an Oval Office press conference on Friday, calling her “really obnoxious.”Video shows Trump speaking as reporters clamored to ask him questions. One woman, later identified as American Urban Radio Networks reporter Ebony McMorris, can be heard asking him about plans to deploy National Guard troops to Memphis.Advertisement
“Quiet,” Trump says in the video, apparently addressing McMorris. “You’re really obnoxious.”“I’m not obnoxious, but I’m trying to ask you, what about your plans for Memphis?” McMorris replies.“You are really obnoxious,” Trump reiterates.Their exchange turns into crosstalk, with McMorris repeating, “I’m not obnoxious, but I am asking, what are your plans for Memphis? Many people wanted to know―”Trump, meanwhile, says, “I’m not going to talk to you until I call on you,” before taking a question from a different reporter.Advertisement
