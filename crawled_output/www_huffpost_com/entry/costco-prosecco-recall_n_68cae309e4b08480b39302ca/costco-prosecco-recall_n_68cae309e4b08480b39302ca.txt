Costco said unopened bottles can shatter even when they’re not being handled or in use.The company said customers should wrap any unopened bottles in paper towels and place them in a plastic bag before disposing of them to avoid any risk from shattered glass.Costco didn’t respond Tuesday when The Associated Press asked if there were any reported injuries due to the issue.
