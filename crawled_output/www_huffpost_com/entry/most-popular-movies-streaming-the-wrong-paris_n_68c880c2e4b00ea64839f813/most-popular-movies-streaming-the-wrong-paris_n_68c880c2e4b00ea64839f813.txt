Only to find out the show is set in Paris... Texas. Her next mission is to get eliminated as fast as possible, but not before the cowboy bachelor (Pierson Fodé) can sweep her off her feet.Advertisement
The movie also stars Yvonne Orji, Madison Pettis, Frances Fisher, Emilija Baranac, Madeleine Arthur, Torrance Coombs, Christin Park and Hannah Stocking.Despite its trending status on Netflix, “The Wrong Paris” has received mixed reviews from critics and viewers, with a 60% Rotten Tomatoes score. One user on X wrote, “GUYS I HAD A BLAST WATCHING THE WRONG PARIS.” Meanwhile, outlets like The Guardian called the Netflix film one of the streamer’s “many low-grade background watches.”Advertisement
Read on for more trending movies across streaming services, including HBO Max, Amazon Prime Video and Apple TV+. And if you want to stay informed about all things streaming and entertainment, subscribe to the Culture Catchall newsletter.‘Warfare’“Warfare” is currently the No. 1 movie on HBO Max.The visceral war drama tells the boots-on-the-ground story of a platoon of Navy SEALs who embark on a dangerous mission in Ramadi during the Iraq War. The film is based on the real-life experiences of former Navy SEAL Ray Mendoza, who co-wrote and directed alongside Alex Garland.Advertisement
The cast includes Will Poulter, Charles Melton, Joseph Quinn, Kit Connor, Cosmo Jarvis, Finn Bennett, D’Pharaoh Woon-A-Tai, Taylor John Smith, Michael Gandolfini, Adain Bradley, Noah Centineo, Evan Holtzman and Henrique Zaga.‘Last Breath’“Last Breath,” a real-life-inspired survival thriller, is one of the top movies on Prime Video right now.Advertisement
The film tells the heart-pounding true story of a seasoned crew of deep-sea divers who battle ocean elements to rescue a crewmate trapped hundreds of feet below the surface. Woody Harrelson, Simu Liu, Finn Cole and Cliff Curtis star in the cast.‘Highest 2 Lowest’“Highest 2 Lowest,” Spike Lee and Denzel Washington’s latest collaboration, is currently the top movie on Apple TV+. The thriller, a modern reimagining of Akira Kurosawa’s “High and Low,” stars Washington as famed music mogul David King, who faces a moral and financial dilemma when he’s targeted for a multi-million dollar ransom plot just as he prepares to buy back his legendary record label.Advertisement
Also starring in the film are A$AP Rocky, Jeffrey Wright, Ilfenesh Hadera, Wendell Pierce, John Douglas Thompson, Dean Winters, Aubrey Joseph and Elijah Wright.“Highest 2 Lowest” earned mixed reviews from audiences after its lackluster rollout sparked backlash online.
