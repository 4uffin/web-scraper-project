Each season revolves around a new murder mystery, and in Season 5, which premiered on Sept. 9, the crime involves the trio’s doorman, Lester (Teddy Coluca).Also starring in Season 5 are Meryl Streep, Da’Vine Joy Randolph, Renée Zellweger, Keegan-Michael Key, Beanie Feldstein, Dianne Wiest, Jermaine Fowler, Christoph Waltz and Logan Lerman.
