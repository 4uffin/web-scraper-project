Log in to hide these messages.Every Winner From The 2025 Primetime Emmys: The Complete ListThe night included several surprise victories and multiple history-making wins.By Kelby VeraSenior ReporterSep 14, 2025, 08:11 PM EDTLEAVE A COMMENTLOADINGERROR LOADINGThe spotlight was on the small screen during the 77th Primetime Emmy Awards on Sunday.Hosted by comedian Nate Bargatze at Los Angeles’ Peacock Theater, the Emmys honored the best shows that streaming platforms, premium cable and network TV had to offer.HBO Max’s edge-of-your-seat hospital drama “The Pitt” won Outstanding Drama Series, beating Apple TV+’s star-packed, big-budget workplace enigma “Severance,” which had more nominations than any other show this cycle.Advertisement
The hysterical Hollywood send-up “The Studio” broke the Emmy record for the most wins for a comedy series with 13 awards.View of Emmy statuettes at the 77th Primetime Emmy Awards at Los Angeles' Peacock Theater on Sunday.Frazer Harrison via Getty ImagesThe four-part Netflix miniseries “Adolescence” won best Limited or Anthology Series, while actor Owen Cooper became the youngest male Emmy winner of all time for playing a 13-year-old boy accused of killing his classmate.Advertisement
