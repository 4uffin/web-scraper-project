WongSenior Culture Reporter, HuffPostSep 23, 2025, 05:14 PM EDTLEAVE A COMMENTLOADINGERROR LOADINGChappell Roan may be known for her edgy style, but she recently found herself caught off-guard by the boldness of her own outfit. Footage of Roan’s Saturday concert in Forest Hills, New York, shows the pop star playfully expressing her dismay when she caught sight of her butt-baring, red-and-black corset ensemble on a Jumbotron inside the venue. “Oh my God, I forgot my bottom was just a thong,” she told the crowd, as seen in a TikTok clip shared by StubHub. “I look up at the screen and see my ass. I was like, it’s gone.”As it turns out, Roan’s look was intentional, and she quickly launched into her next song as the audience roared its approval. Roan, 27, is wrapping her breakout year on her Visions of Damsels & Other Dangerous Things Tour. The trek kicked off in Warsaw, Poland, on May 30 and is currently set to wrap on Nov. 14 in Mexico City.
