Online court records don’t list an attorney representing him.Marine One was airborne on Saturday near the White House when a U.S. Secret Service patrol officer spotted Winkler walking on a sidewalk, shirtless and loudly talking to himself, the officer wrote in an affidavit.Donald Trump boards Marine One as he departs from the South Lawn of the White House on Sept. 22, 2025.SAUL LOEB via Getty ImagesThe officer said he shone a flashlight at Winkler, who apparently retaliated by flashing a red laser beam at the officer’s face.Advertisement
As Marine One flew over their heads, Winkler looked up and shined the laser pointer at the helicopter, according to the officer.
