Correction to Ben & Jerry's Article
Provided by Dow Jones
Sep 17, 2025, 7:15:00 AM
A spokesman for the Magnum Ice Cream Company said in a statement they were grateful for Jerry Greenfield's role in founding the company. "Ben & Jerry's Co-Founder Greenfield Quits Ahead of Unilever's Ice-Cream Spinoff," at 0658 GMT, incorrectly attributed the comment to a spokeswoman.
