Workday Up Nearly 9%, Best Performer in the S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100 So Far Today — Data Talk
Provided by Dow Jones
Sep 17, 2025, 2:49:00 PM
Workday, Inc. Class A (WDAY) is currently at $238.29, up $19.28 or 8.8%
--Would be highest close since July 29, 2025, when it closed at $239.63
--On pace for largest percent increase since April 9, 2025, when it rose 9.95%
--Snaps a three day losing streak
--Up 3.24% month-to-date
--Down 7.65% year-to-date
--Down 22.43% from its all-time closing high of $307.21 on Feb. 26, 2024
--Down 4.10% from 52 weeks ago (Sept. 18, 2024), when it closed at $248.47
--Down 14.87% from its 52-week closing high of $279.91 on Dec. 9, 2024
--Up 13.22% from its 52-week closing low of $210.47 on April 8, 2025
--Traded as high as $240.63; highest intraday level since July 30, 2025, when it hit $240.92
--Up 9.87% at today's intraday high; largest intraday percent increase since April 9, 2025, when it rose as much as 10.52%
--Best performer in the S&P 500 today
--Best performer in the Nasdaq 100 today
