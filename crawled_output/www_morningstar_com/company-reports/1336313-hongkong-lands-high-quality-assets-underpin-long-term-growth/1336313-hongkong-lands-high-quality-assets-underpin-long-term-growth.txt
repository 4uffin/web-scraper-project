Business Strategy and Outlook Hongkong Land develops, grows, and holds a portfolio of high-quality assets in Hong Kong’s central business district. Over more than a century, it assiduously assembled and upgraded these assets and turned them into a portfolio consisting of the most desirable office and retail locations in the city. The Central portfolio accounts for nearly 65% of the company’s operating profit.
