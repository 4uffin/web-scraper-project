Key Takeaways
Local searches drive nearly half of all Google queries, and now AI is making it easier than ever for small businesses to capture that demand.
Combining local marketing with AI allows small businesses to compete with and even outperform big brands through speed, relevance and personalization.
To implement local marketing + AI, you must audit your local presence, use AI to automate smartly and layer in local intent everywhere. You must also measure, test and refine your results.
