Meet the team
Our story
In the heart of IT World, amidst the humming of tech giants and the buzz of innovation, our story began. It was a tale of passion, perseverance, and a relentless drive to revolutionize the digital landscape.
Born from the shared vision of a group of ambitious entrepreneurs, our software company emerged with a singular mission: to redefine the way businesses harness technology to thrive in the modern world. Armed with nothing but dreams and determination, we set out on a journey that would shape the future of industries far and wide.
From front-end tech to back-end we embraced emerging trends that shape the future of technology.
Our stack
• Frontend: TypeScript, JavaScript, React.JS, Next.JS, Svelte.JS, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap
• UI Tools: Tailwind CSS, Material UI, Reshaped, Reactstrap, Ant Design, Skeleton UI, Melt UI
• State Management: Redux, Redux Thunk, Redux Saga, Zustand, Axios, Fetch, Graphql
• Data Visualization: Recharts, React-table
• Code Analysis: Eslint, Prettier, SonarCloud
• Testing: Cypress, Testcafe, Jest, React-testing-library
• Backend: Node.JS
• Database: Firebase, Firestore, FaunaDB, MongoDB
• DevOps: CI/CD integration, Vercel, Netlify, Auth0, Clerk, Okta
• Other: Rest APIs, Monolithic & Micro-frontends, Bit
