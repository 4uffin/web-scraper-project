Microsoft Tag Rules
To ensure the Microsoft tag remains a valuable and positive space for discussion and learning, please follow these guidelines:
Stay On Topic: Ensure your posts and comments are relevant to Microsoft technologies, products, or services.
Be Respectful: Treat all members with respect and kindness. No harassment, discrimination, or inflammatory comments.
No Spam: Refrain from posting spammy content or self-promotion without contributing to the discussion.
