about #adventofcode
Advent of Code is a set of challenges created by Eric Wastl that appear 1 per day (2 parts each) between December 1st and 25th that are Christmas-themed and are intended to be solved with code.
These posts are about community solutions, the challenges, and the competition as a whole.
