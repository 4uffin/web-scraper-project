Power Apps - Microsoft Power Platform
Follow
Hide
Power Apps are a Low Code application builder and is part of Microsoft's Power Platform. There are 2 version, model and canvas.
Model are built from database tables built in Dataverse, with minimum UI alterations but powerful data integration.
Canvas Apps are fully customisable with a WYSIWYG interface. Canvas app functionality is extended with PowerFX, the expression based language for the Power Platform
Bother versions simplify business app development, allowing users to build, share, and use apps tailored to their specific needs.

In articles about Power Apps, readers can expect to discover how this Microsoft platform enables rapid app development, revolutionizing business processes.
Topics often include tutorials on creating apps without coding, integrating with data sources, and maximizing productivity. Case studies highlight real-world applications, while insights into the evolving capabilities and future trends in app development keep readers informed and inspired.
Whether for business solutions or personal projects, Power Apps offers a versatile toolbox worth exploring.
