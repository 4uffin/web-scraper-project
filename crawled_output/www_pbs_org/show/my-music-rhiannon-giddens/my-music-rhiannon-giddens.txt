Read MoreSeason 3 of "My Music with Rhiannon Giddens” accompanies North Carolina's celebrated native daughter Rhiannon Giddens around her adopted home country of Ireland as she interviews and performs with some outstanding current stars of Irish traditional music.
Season 3 guests include Róisín Elsafty, Loah, Enda Scahill, Emer Mayock, Niwel Tsumbu and Karan Casey.
