Job Details
Sanhang Institute for Brain Science and Technology (SiBST) was launched in September 2023 to fill in this research gap in China. The Chinese word ‘Sanhang’ in the name covers aeronautics, aerospace and marine science all together. The institute aims to combine brain research with these areas, with the short-term mission of sending laboratory rodents into deep space and the eventual ambition of preparing humans for living for long periods in deep space or the deep ocean. We are recruiting high-level and outstanding young talents globally and welcome them to join our university.
Chair Professor
Position Requirements: Lead international scientific frontiers, achieve systematic and innovative accomplishments in basic research, and attain landmark scientific achievements in major national science and technology missions. Typically, candidates should be strategic scientists with high social reputation and broad academic influence.
Support Benefits: Case-by-case negotiation, personalized support based on needs.
Leading Talent
Position Requirements: Conduct pioneering and leading research in their discipline, gaining high recognition from peers domestically and internationally. Make significant contributions to social development and scientific progress in line with major national needs. Typically, candidates should be national-level leading talents who have made major contributions in discipline construction, scientific research, talent cultivation, etc.
Support Benefits: Case-by-case negotiation, personalized support based on needs.
Young Talent
Position Requirements: Hold a doctoral degree and be under 40 years of age. Have held a formal teaching or research position at a renowned university, research institution, or corporate R&D department domestically or internationally. Generally, candidates should be outstanding young talents who have achieved high academic accomplishments in their field and possess the potential to become leading talents in the field.
Support Benefits:
Appointment: Tenured position, appointed as Professor and Doctoral Supervisor with a senior professional title.
Compensation: Competitive annual salary + relocation allowance and monetary subsidies + performance bonuses + other income such as outstanding contribution rewards, with no upper limit. Additional rewards and support from the school (research institute).
Research Support: Ample start-up research funding. Guaranteed one doctoral student quota annually for the first three years. Full salary coverage for postdoctoral researchers recruited within the first three years.
Living Benefits: Children enjoy access to NPU's high-quality educational resources (kindergarten through high school, nationally top-ranked). Eligibility for the "Sanqin Excellence Card," providing VIP services at provincial airports and railway stations, "green channel" access in the healthcare system, health check-ups at top-tier hospitals, free entry to A-level scenic spots in the province, and other exclusive services for high-level talents.
Special Notes: Candidates who reach the interview stage for the Overseas Outstanding Young Talents program but are not selected may be included in the NPU Aoxiang Young Fellow program through certain procedures. Applicants for the Overseas Outstanding Young Talents program may simultaneously apply for the "Shaanxi Provincial Talent Program" and the NPU Aoxiang Young Fellow program. Those selected for both the national Overseas Outstanding Young Talents program and the provincial talent program will receive the higher level of support. Exceptionally outstanding candidates in relevant fields will receive additional support from the "Aoxiang Talent (Special Funding) Fund."
NPU Aoxiang Young Fellow
Position Requirements: Hold a doctoral degree; be under 40 years of age；Meet the academic standards for NPU's tenure-track professor position; have demonstrated potential in their field and possess the ability to grow into a national-level young talent.
Support Benefits:
Appointment: Tenured position; appointed as Tenure-Track Professor (senior professional title) and, through certain procedures, as Doctoral Supervisor.
Compensation: Competitive annual salary + performance bonuses + other income such as research rewards + personalized school incentives, with no upper limit.
Research Support: Ample start-up research funding.
Living Benefits: Relocation subsidy. Children enjoy access to NPU's high-quality educational resources (kindergarten through high school, nationally top-ranked).
Special Notes: Candidates who reach the interview stage for the Overseas Outstanding Young Talents program but are not selected may be included in the NPU Aoxiang Young Fellow position through certain procedures and receive corresponding benefits.
Tenure-Track Professor and Tenure-Track Associate Professor
Position Requirements: Tenure-Track Professor should be under 40 years of age; Tenure-Track Associate Professor should be under 35 years of age. Meet the academic standards for the respective position.
Support Benefits:
Appointment: Tenured position; Tenure-Track Professor receives a senior professional title, while Tenure-Track Associate Professor receives an associate senior professional title.
Compensation: Competitive annual salary, performance bonuses, other income such as research rewards, personalized school incentives.
Research Support: Ample start-up research funding.
Living Benefits: Relocation subsidy. Children enjoy access to NPU's high-quality educational resources (kindergarten through high school, nationally top-ranked).
Postdoctoral Researcher (Special Funding Postdoc/Regular Postdoc)
Position Requirements: Within 3 years of obtaining a doctoral degree. Special Funding Postdoc should be under 32 years of age and meet the academic level of Assistant Professor. Regular Postdoc should be under 35 years of age.
Benefits:
Compensation: Competitive annual salary, personalized school incentives, additional income such as national or provincial postdoctoral project funding.
Career Development: During the term, those who meet the standards for Tenure-Track Associate Professor or higher may apply for a pre-appointment as a tenure-track faculty member. Upon appointment, they will be managed under the corresponding position and receive corresponding benefits.
Interested candidates are encouraged to submit their CV to Prof. Jun Chen (junchen_sibst@nwpu.edu.cn).
