This one's on us. We didn't get it done in time. Thanks to Comedy Central and South Park fans for being so understanding. Tune in next week!"The two also provided fans with a revised schedule for the rest of the season.Following Ep. 5 next Wednesday, fans will have to wait three more weeks before Ep. 6 airs on Oct. 15.From there, the show will resume it's usual every-other-week release schedule for episodes 7-10, which are set for Nov. 12, Nov. 26, and Dec. 10 respectively.Loading twitter content...
