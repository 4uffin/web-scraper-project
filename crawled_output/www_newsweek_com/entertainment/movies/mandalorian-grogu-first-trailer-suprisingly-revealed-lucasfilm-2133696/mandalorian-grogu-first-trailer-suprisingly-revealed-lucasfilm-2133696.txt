Now, there should be more excitement for the film, as Lucasfilm decided to drop the first trailer for the film out of nowhere.The trailer does not showcase any dialogue, but there is plenty of action. Din Djarin and Grogu are back together and appearing to get into plenty of hijinks.What is interesting about the trailer is that it showcases some new faces, one of which is Sigourney Weaver, who is set to make her Star Wars debut. The character made famous by "Star Wars Rebels" was one of the shocking live-action debuts in "The Mandalorian."From the looks of the trailer, Zeb is also getting into some sort of fight.
