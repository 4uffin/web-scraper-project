GolfRyder CupNews ArticleRyder Cup 2025: Tee Times and Full Match Schedule at Bethpage BlackPublishedSep 22, 2025 at 08:24 AM EDTBy Devlina SarkarContributing Sports WriterShareNewsweek is a Trust Project memberTwo years ago, Team Europe clinched a commanding 16.5-11.5 victory at Marco Simone in Rome, lifting the Ryder Cup for the 12th time.Now, the showdown returns to America at Bethpage Black in New York, with high expectations from Team UA and a fired-up crowd.For the first time in Ryder Cup history, American players will be paid to play the foursomes and fourball matches, marking a major shift in tradition.While the move has stirred debate across the golf world, it adds another layer of pressure for the pros, who are already tipped as favorites....If Team USA reaches 14.5 points on Sunday, it would mark their 28th Ryder Cup win. The odds are stacked in their favor at -140, while Europe trails at +150.The Ryder Cup week begins with four practice days from Monday to Thursday, featuring player interviews, team strategy sessions, and the fan-favorite All-Star Match on Wednesday, which tees off at 7:45 a.m. ET.Adding to the intrigue, the Junior Ryder Cup also unfolds from Sept. 23-25, with matches played across Nassau Country Club and Bethpage Black.The Opening Ceremony kicks off Thursday at 4 p.m. ET, setting the tone for Friday's first tee shot and what promises to be a fierce three-day battle at New York.Ryder Cup 2025
