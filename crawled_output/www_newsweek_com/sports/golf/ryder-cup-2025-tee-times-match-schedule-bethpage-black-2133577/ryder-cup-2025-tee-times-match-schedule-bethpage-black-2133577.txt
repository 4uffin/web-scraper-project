Two years ago, Team Europe clinched a commanding 16.5-11.5 victory at Marco Simone in Rome, lifting the Ryder Cup for the 12th time.Now, the showdown returns to America at Bethpage Black in New York, with high expectations from Team UA and a fired-up crowd.For the first time in Ryder Cup history, American players will be paid to play the foursomes and fourball matches, marking a major shift in tradition.While the move has stirred debate across the golf world, it adds another layer of pressure for the pros, who are already tipped as favorites.
