legitimate rushing attack plus a banged up wide receiver corps is a big reason why.Quarterback Patrick Mahomes is the team’s leading rusher (125 yards, two touchdowns), largely because the Chiefs’ banged-up receiver group have had a tough time getting open.More NFL: Chiefs’ Andy Reid Doesn’t Hold Back on His Sideline Confrontation With Travis KelceMarquise Brown has been solid (27 targets, 19 that though Rice is still suspended through Week 6, he’s now allowed back at the team’s training facility following Week 3.Rice still can’t practice with his teammates, but having him back in team meetings is something head coach Andy Reid said his players have been looking forward to.More NFL: Falcons Shake Up Coaching Staff After Embarrassing Blowout vs PanthersLoading twitter content...“We and just having him around, he’s a good, positive energy to have around.”Rice, who played just four games last season before suffering a torn LCL after colliding with Mahomes during a game against the Los Angeles Chargers, was suspended for the first six weeks of the season after violating the NFL’s personal conduct policy.The 25-year-old Rice led all Chiefs receivers in receptions (79), targets
