"The Queen of Spades" appeared in a backstage segment at the "NXT Homecoming" special on Tuesday, September 16.The return comes after Baszler was reportedly working in a backstage producer role for the brand in July. Her on-screen appearance now opens the door for a potential in-ring comeback.Loading twitter content...A Backstage ConfrontationDuring the "Homecoming" show, which took place at NXT's original home of Full Sail University, Baszler appeared in a backstage segment with two of the brand's rising stars, Sol Ruca and Zaria. After a recent miscommunication in a tag team match, the two were arguing when Baszler stepped in to confront them."The Queen of Spades" reminded them of her own decorated past as a two-time NXT Women's Champion and a WWE Women's Tag Team Champion, telling them to get their act together. The segment positioned Baszler as a veteran leader and a potential mentor or antagonist for the younger talent.A Dominant But Abrupt WWE RunShayna Baszler's release from WWE in May was a surprise to many, especially after she revealed in a recent interview that she had three years remaining on her contract at the time. A pioneer of women's mixed martial arts, she brought a unique level of legitimate credibility to the WWE women's division....Her run in NXT was one of the most dominant in the brand's history. As a two-time NXT Women's Champion, she held the title for a combined 549 days, establishing herself as a feared and respected final boss.More news: WWE Superstar Announces Retirement MatchWhile she went on to become a multi-time WWE Women's Tag Team Champion on the main roster, many fans felt her intimidating aura was never fully utilized on "Raw" or "SmackDown."
