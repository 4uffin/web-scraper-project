U.S.Breaking NewsMass Shootingactive shooterGeorgiaNews ArticleAugusta Mall Shooting: Active Shooter Confirmed in GeorgiaPublishedSep 23, 2025 at 02:27 PM EDTupdatedSep 23, 2025 at 05:49 PM EDTBy Dan Gooding and Hannah ParryShareNewsweek is a Trust Project memberOne person is reportedly dead after a shooter opened fire at the Augusta Mall in Georgia, the Richmond County Sheriff’s Office (RCSO) confirmed on Tuesday afternoon.The department said that it has two people in custody, according to Atlanta news station 11Alive.In an update on Facebook, the RCSO said deputies were searching the mall one store at a time for any other possible injured shoppers or staff.Brookfield Properties, which runs the mall, told Newsweek that the incident was isolated to a group of people. The mall would be closed for the rest of the day, a spokesperson said. Why It MattersTuesday's death marked the third shooting at the mall in less than 1 1/2 years. One person was injured by gunfire inside Dillard's at the mall in April 2024, while a man was harmed in another shooting at the mall in June 2024.What To KnowThe first reports came in around 1:25 p.m. local time of multiple gunshots. Staff and customers at the mall were instructed to shelter in place while the shopping center was placed under lockdown, according to Augusta news station WRDW.Police and emergency crews remained on the scene, with unconfirmed local reports of multiple potential victims. At least three stretchers were taken to the scene.It was later reported by WRDW that one person had died. The RCSO was expected to give further details later in the day.The RCSO did say that deputies were searching for one person in the shooting and told residents that the operation would likely take some time.Shoppers and staff speaking to local media described hiding in dressing rooms while they waited for police to arrive and safely escort them from the building. What People Are SayingRCSO, in an update on Facebook: "We are still on scene dealing with multiple issues at this time, and still searching for one individual. One person shot in total. Deputies are having to clear store by store to look for anybody that is still inside hiding and or possibly having a medical emergency. This process will take a while, so we thank you for your patience and cooperation in advance!"What Happens NextFurther updates are expected on the suspect.
