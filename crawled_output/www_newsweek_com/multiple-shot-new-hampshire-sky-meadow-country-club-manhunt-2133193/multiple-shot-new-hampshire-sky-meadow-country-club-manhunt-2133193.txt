Video surveillance helped authorities identify and detain the lone gunman, according to the Nashua Police Department.Police have declared there is no ongoing threat to public safety but emphasized that the shooting remains under active investigation.Newsweek reached out to the Nashua Police Department via online form on Saturday for comment.Why It MattersThis shooting represents a significant violent incident in New Hampshire's second-largest city, located approximately 45 miles northwest of Boston along the Massachusetts border.The attack at a recreational facility typically considered a safe community gathering space has shaken residents across the region.Loading twitter content...What To KnowThe incident prompted a massive emergency response, with aerial footage from WMUR-TV showing numerous first responders converging on the scene. Authorities have not released information about victim conditions or the total number of people injured in the attack.Sky Meadow Country Club is a popular recreational facility in Nashua that serves members from both New Hampshire and Massachusetts due to its border location. The club provides golf, dining, and event hosting services for the local community.The swift police response and use of video surveillance technology proved crucial in identifying and apprehending the shooter. Initial confusion about the number of suspects has been clarified through security footage analysis, helping authorities provide accurate information to the public.Nashua sits strategically along the New Hampshire-Massachusetts border, making it easily accessible to residents from both states. The city's proximity to Boston makes it a popular destination for recreational activities and business events.The Nashua Police Department is asking the public to avoid the Sky Meadow area while investigators process the crime scene....What People Are SayingNashua Police Department on X: "Video surveillance confirmed there was one shooter, and they are the person being detained by police. The scene is still an active investigation, but there is no further danger to the public."U.S. Rep. Maggie Goodlander said in a statement: "I am closely monitoring the tragic reports of a shooting tonight at Sky Meadow Country Club in Nashua.
