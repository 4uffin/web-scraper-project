"Transgender persons are a vulnerable segment of society, and we must all give them dignity and respect," Sindh's provincial chief minister Syed Murad Ali Shah said in a statement.In 2018, Pakistan's Senate voted to support a bill protecting the rights of transgender people, and allowing them to determine their own gender identity.The Transgender Rights Act was praised by many globally, but key sections were later revoked by a sharia court.
