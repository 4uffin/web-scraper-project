Over the past decade, we have been systematically moving elements of the Nasdaq portfolio into the cloud. As we look to redefine the capital markets of the future and tackle transformation of our critical infrastructure for the decades ahead, we are committed to creating an interconnected gateway in the cloud.
Nasdaq's Journey to the Cloud
Over a decade ago, we embarked on our journey to the cloud, starting with foundational data storage, T+1 reporting, and billing workloads. Since then, we’ve systematically moved closer to the matching engine and moved ultra-low latency trading to the cloud.
