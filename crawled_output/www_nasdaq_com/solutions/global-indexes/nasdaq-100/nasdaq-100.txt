Nasdaq-100®: The Index of Innovation
The index caters to investors interested in innovative, growth-oriented companies like Apple, Microsoft, Amazon, and others, offering a diverse selection of industry leaders driving economic progress and technological advancement.

Nasdaq-100® Methodology
What is the Nasdaq-100 Index®?
The Nasdaq-100 Index (NDX®) is a cornerstone of modern investing. The index represents 100 of the largest, most dynamic non-financial companies listed on the Nasdaq Stock Market and some of the most innovative companies in the world. These companies are selected based on market capitalization and are renowned for their innovation, market leadership, and growth potential. NDX includes major players across various sectors such as technology, healthcare, consumer goods, and industrials, making it a comprehensive benchmark for growth-focused investors.Nasdaq-100® Companies ->Nasdaq-100® Product Guide ->

Nasdaq-100® FAQs
How does the Nasdaq-100® differ from other indexes?
Unlike broader market indexes, the Nasdaq-100 specifically excludes financial companies to focus on innovative, non-financial sectors driving modern economic growth. It is heavily weighted towards technology and growth-oriented companies, setting it apart as an index that captures the performance of industry leaders at the forefront of innovation.
Who can benefit from investing in the Nasdaq-100®?
A wide range of investors can benefit, including asset managers, asset owners like pension funds and endowments, insurance companies, and financial advisors. The index offers growth potential and diversification, making it suitable for those seeking exposure to leading companies across multiple high-growth sectors.
How can asset managers utilize the Nasdaq-100®?
Asset managers can leverage the Nasdaq-100® to create differentiated investment products that stand out in a crowded market. By offering funds or ETFs based on the index, they provide clients with access to high-growth, market-leading companies. Nasdaq also offers analytics tools, research, and ongoing support to help optimize investment strategies and enhance performance.
How does the Nasdaq-100® help asset owners diversify their portfolios?
Asset owners can use the Nasdaq-100® to diversify beyond traditional investment allocations. The index provides exposure to leading companies across various high-growth sectors, helping to mitigate risks associated with market volatility and achieve a balance between growth potential and risk management.
What advantages does the Nasdaq-100® offer to insurance companies?
Insurance companies can develop innovative, index-linked annuity products based on the Nasdaq-100®. Aligning with the Nasdaq-100® enhances brand recognition and credibility. Additionally, Nasdaq provides resources and educational materials to support sales teams and advisors, helping them understand and promote the benefits of Nasdaq-100®-linked products.
How can financial advisors leverage the Nasdaq-100® for their clients?
Financial advisors can use the Nasdaq-100® to construct portfolios that offer clients exposure to high-growth, innovative companies. The index's diversified composition allows advisors to tailor investment strategies to meet individual client goals. Nasdaq also provides research, analytics, and educational resources to help advisors stay informed and add value for their clients.
