The Great Shift: Understanding SMA to ETF Conversions in Modern Investing
Explore the growing trend of separately managed account (SMA) to ETF conversions in today's dynamic investment landscape. This comprehensive session brings together three industry leaders who will share their expert perspectives on the transformative market development of the 351 exchange. ETF Architect’s Patrick Cleary will bring technical expertise in ETF structure and implementation, Practus’ Robert Elwood will provide legal and regulatory insights and GTS’ Dave Pennella will offer market making and trading perspectives - all moderated by Nasdaq's Alison Hennessy.
Jan 6, 2025

A Marketing Exchange: How a Dynamic Marketing and Communication Strategy Can Define ETF Success
While bringing an ETF to market can be complex, generating engaging marketing campaigns to effectively promote a new fund is challenging without a thoughtful strategy and execution model. Dive into ETF marketing with Nasdaq’s Elizabeth Coogan, Defiance Analytics’ Jacob Ingram, F/m Investments’ Courtney Sheehy and Craft & Capital’s Chris Sullivan.

New Frontiers for ETFs: Converting Investment Vehicles and Impacts of Share Class Proposals
Which investment vehicles have the potential to make the best ETFs? Panelists from Nasdaq, K&L Gates, Brown Brothers Harriman, and Cohen & Company speak about the various products that can convert to an ETF, as well as the ins and outs of the conversion process and discuss the possible impacts of the multi-share class proposals.
