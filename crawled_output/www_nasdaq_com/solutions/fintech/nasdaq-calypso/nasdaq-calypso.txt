Capital Markets Technology
​​A ​Versatile ​Foundation with​​ Solutions​ Built to Scale
Drawing on decades of expertise in capital markets and banking, Nasdaq Calypso is purpose-built for efficient, robust, and transparent capital markets ​operations across a broad spectrum of exchange-traded and OTC asset classes. The platform integrates front-, middle-, and back-office solutions to streamline and simplify your operations from end to end, incorporating centralized data management, risk analytics, compliance and reporting to enable your firm to thrive in an ever-changing reform and regulatory environment.
Regardless of the business case, our robust solutions enable agile business development with the highest standards for resilience, performance, and security.
Strengthen Your Technology Foundation for Tomorrow
Available on-premises, deployed in the cloud or delivered via SaaS, our open, extensible architecture, robust and centralized data management and advanced straight-through processing ensure efficiency, scalability, flexibility and interoperability to meet tomorrow’s needs with a modern, resilient foundation.
Support the Full Trade Lifecycle on One Platform
Our interoperable, modular solutions facilitate modernization of the trade lifecycle and supporting infrastructure, from pre-trade simulation, front-office trading, hedging and funding, risk management, P&L compliance and limits, through to clearing, collateral management and post-trade processing, holistically for cleared and uncleared trades across asset classes.
Rely on a Trusted Partner
As a highly-regulated organization and market operator, Nasdaq’s technology is trusted to power mission-critical operations at over 3,500 financial institutions – including financial regulators, central banks, financial market infrastructures and its own high-performance markets worldwide.
Benefit from More Than Technology
In addition to continuous large-scale investments in innovation and developing our technology to meet new and evolving market demands, Nasdaq’s Global Operations, Client Experience, Advisory and Learning Services teams help you successfully operate and grow your business.
