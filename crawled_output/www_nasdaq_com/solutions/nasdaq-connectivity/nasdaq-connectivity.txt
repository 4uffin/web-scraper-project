Connectivity Services
Connect to Nasdaq Markets
Nasdaq is driven to continually enhance the tools you need to trade. Nasdaq offers a wide variety of connectivity options and market data to fit a firm's specific network and trading strategy needs. Nasdaq offers Co-location, Point of Presence (POP) and Connectivity services for low latency and access to our liquid markets.
Types of Connectivity Services
NDX®
Nasdaq-100 Index® Options
The underlying value of NDX index options is equal to the full value of the Nasdaq-100 Index (NDX). NDX index options offer both monthly AM settlement and weekly PM settlement to fit various investor needs.

Nasdaq-100 Index® Options
NDX®
The underlying value of NDX index options is equal to the full value of the Nasdaq-100 Index (NDX). NDX index options offer both monthly AM settlement and weekly PM settlement to fit various investor needs.
Learn more->
XND®
Nasdaq-100 Micro Index® Options
XND® index options are based on 1/100th (1%) the full value of the Nasdaq-100 Index (NDX). XND index options offer monthly and weekly PM settlement, providing a manageable way to hedge or gain NDX exposure.
