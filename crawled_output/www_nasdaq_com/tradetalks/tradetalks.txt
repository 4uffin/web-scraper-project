Meet Our Host
Jill Malandrino
Host of TradeTalks
Jill Malandrino is a creator and host of Nasdaq TradeTalks, which broadcasts live from the Nasdaq MarketSite and locations around the globe. Through insightful panel discussions with cross-sector thought leaders, executives and entrepreneurs, regulators and policymakers, as well as academia and legal experts, she provides viewers with a comprehensive understanding of global capital markets, emerging technologies, digital assets and the regulatory environment.In addition to her work as host of Nasdaq TradeTalks, Jill moderates fireside chats and serves as a panelist at marquee events worldwide, including the Singapore FinTech Festival, SALT, CES, Consensus, Mainnet, Rally and Exchange ETFs.Prior to joining Nasdaq, Jill was a reporter with more than a decade of experience. She started her career in the institutional equity markets, which provided a robust foundation for navigating the dynamic landscape of financial and emerging technology media.Jill holds a master's degree in Communication and Media from Rutgers University, a Bachelor of Arts degree in Journalism from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst, and is attending Seton Hall University School of Law, pursuing a Master of Legal Studies degree in Privacy Law and Cyber Security.
