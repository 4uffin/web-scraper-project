Are you baffled by the missing Key of Apostate in Hollow Knight: Silksong? This particular item is in a far-flung region, a location which you might not expect. Getting there, naturally, takes a bit of effort. Our guide has all the details to help you obtain what you seek.Table of Contents [hide]Where to find the Key of Apostate in Hollow Knight: Silksong - The Slab locked doors guideKey of Apostate locationWhat lies beyond the Key of Apostate rooms Where to find the Key of Apostate in Hollow Knight: Silksong - The Slab locked doors guideThe Slab is one of the regions of Pharloom and you should definitely visit it if you want to obtain the Faydown Cloak double jump ability. While you're exploring, you're bound to pick up items like the Key of Indolent and Key of Heretic. However, you'll also notice that other doors remain locked and they require the Key of Apostate.Key of Apostate locationThe Hollow Knight: Silksong Key of Apostate can be found in the Putrefied Ducts region, which is actually in the top-right corner of the world map:You can either take the lower-right exit from Memorium or the topmost exit from Bilewater.Putrefied Ducts has numerous hazards, including carnivorous vines that pull at gobble up Hornet. Once you reach the zone, you need to go to the lower-left section of the zone. It's also where you'll meet the Huntress NPC for the Broodfeast quest.The Key of Apostate is in a small nook at the upper-left corner of the chamber.You have to go all the way to the Putrefied Ducts just to obtain the Key of Apostate.What lies beyond the Key of Apostate roomsOnce you have the Hollow Knight: Silksong Key of Apostate, make your way back to The Slab. You should now be able to unlock the two remaining doorways:The door at the bottom of the shaft--i.e. the one that's closest to the bench and fast travel point--leads to an eerie chamber with a cell with multiple chains. If you break these chains, you can battle the First Sinner boss. Defeating her nets you the Rune Rage silk skill.The door at the top-right portion of the shaft has a fairly annoying jumping/traversal segment. Your goal is to pogo on the spinning bladed wheel. Once you get to the very top, you'll find a Mask Shard health upgrade.Gallery
The First Sinner boss fight (left) and the jumping challenge for the Mask Shard (right) are sure to test your mettle. That does it for our guide on where to find the Key of Apostate in Hollow Knight: Silksong.
