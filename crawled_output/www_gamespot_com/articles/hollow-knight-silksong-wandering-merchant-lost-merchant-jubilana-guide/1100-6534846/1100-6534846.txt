Are you perplexed as to how you'd be able to complete Wandering Merchant in Hollow Knight: Silksong? This quest, along with Lost Merchant, concern an NPC named Jubilana, who seems to have encountered some mishaps while exploring the Citadel. Saving her is of paramount importance, as she sells some amazing goodies.Table of Contents [hide]How to complete Wandering Merchant and Lost Merchant in Hollow Knight: Silksong - Jubilana locations guideWhere to find Jubilana for the Wandering Merchant and Lost Merchant quests?What can you purchase from Jubilana?Other notable rewardsHow to complete Wandering Merchant and Lost Merchant in Hollow Knight: Silksong - Jubilana locations guideThe Wandering Merchant and Lost Merchant quest come from the wish board in Songclave, a small settlement at the far-right corner of Choral Chamber. Jubilana's quests won't appear in the settlement initially. Instead, you must do the following:Ring the bell in Songclave and talk to the Caretaker.Fast travel out of Songclave and return later.Tackle a few side quests from the wish board while you're at it.Jubilana can be found in different locations depending on the quest that you're currently undertaking.Where to find Jubilana for the Wandering Merchant and Lost Merchant quests?The map above shows Jubilana's locations for the Hollow Knight: Silksong Wandering Merchant and Lost Merchant quests. Here's a quick summary:Wandering Merchant appears earlier on the Songclave wish board--i.e. around the halfway point of Act 2.Jubilana is at the left-hand side of Choral Chamber--i.e. above and to the left of the entrance to Whiteward. Take out the enemies in the room so she knows that it's safe.The Lost Merchant quest, meanwhile, only appears much later in the campaign. You might need to complete other side quests from the Songclave board, including Fine Pins, Balm for the Wounded, and others.Once you're able to pick up the quest, go to the Memorium zone and head all the way to the lower-right corner. There's a hidden path that lets you access an open area leading to Putrefied Ducts. Jubilana is surrounded by several flying enemies, so just defeat the hostiles until the coast is clear.What can you purchase from Jubilana?Jubilana tends to have a bunch of new items up for grabs when you complete her related quests. Upon completing the Hollow Knight: Silksong Wandering Merchant quest, you'll see the following unique wares:Tool: Ascendant's Grip - Costs 350x RosariesTool: Spool Extender - Costs 720x RosariesHealth upgrade material: Mask Shard - Costs 750x RosariesUnique item: Simple Key - Costs 650x RosariesLater, once you've finished Lost Merchant, you'll see the following in her inventory:Tool: Spider Strings - Costs 320x Rosaries.Silk upgrade material: Spool Fragment - Costs 500x RosariesCrafting currency: Craftmetal - Costs 180x RosariesQuest item: Choral Commandment - Costs 210x RosariesAmass enough Rosaries so you can purchase all of Jubilana's wares.Other notable rewardsThat does it for our guide on how to complete Wandering Merchant and Lost Merchant in Hollow Knight: Silksong. The items up for grabs aren't the only rewards that await you if you save Jubilana.That's because her quests, along with those from the bounty boards in Bone Bottom and Bellhart, are required to trigger a surprising turn of events later in the campaign. You can learn more about these details in our Silk and Soul/Act 3 guide.For other side quests, you can take a look at our Broodfeast guide, Alchemist's Assistant guide, and Great Taste of Pharloom guide.Play as Hornet and brave the dangers of the Kingdom of Pharloom.
