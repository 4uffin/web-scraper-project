Wizards of the Coast routinely releases a special limited-time Secret Lair drop as a fundraiser for Extra Life--the gaming-centric charity that supports Children's Miracle Network hospitals--ahead of the charity's annual 24-hour gaming marathon. This year will be no different, but the drop will feature a unique twist: Play-Doh.The drop, called Secret Lair x Play-Doh: Squishful Thinking, features reprinted cards sporting artwork taken from real-life Play-Doh sculptures.
