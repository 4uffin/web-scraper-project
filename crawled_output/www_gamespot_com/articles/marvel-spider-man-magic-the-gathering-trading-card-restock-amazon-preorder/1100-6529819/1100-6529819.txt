Spider-Man Magic: The Gathering Bundle & Scene Box Restocked Ahead Of Friday's LaunchMagic: The Gathering's crossover with Marvel's Spider-Man releases September 26, but most products have been sold out for months.By
Steven Petite
on September 25, 2025 at 5:42PM PDT 3 Comments
Magic: The Gathering - Marvel's Spider-Man Set
Releases September 26
See full set at Amazon
Amazon has restocked two of the long sold-out Spider-Man Magic: The Gathering products ahead of this Friday's launch (September 26). Spidey's Spectacular Showdown Scene Box is in stock $44.87, and the Marvel's Spider-Man Bundle is back down to $70 after Amazon initially restocked it at $85.66. The pair join the Spider-Man Play Booster Box as the only in-stock products in Magic: The Gathering's popular crossover set. The Daily Bugle Breaking News set features five cards with newspaper covers. Naturally, J. Jonah Jameson's headlines aren't very flattering for Spidey. The second set is called Heroic Deeds and features Spider-Man, Miles Morales, and Gwen Stacy saving the day. Each Secret Lair set comes in foil and non-foil editions for $40 and $30, respectively. Marvel's Spider-Man - Spidey's Spectacular Showdown Scene Box
$44.87 | In stock
Spidey's Spectacular Showdown Scene Box includes three Marvel's Spider-Man booster packs as well as two different sets of six scene cards. You'll get six Traditional Foil Borderless Scene Cards that you can use when playing the card game. The artwork on those cards is also found on six Art-Only Scene Cards. The art cards aren't playable, but the Scene Box comes with a display easel to showcase the artwork.
