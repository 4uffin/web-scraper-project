After a run in early access, Supergiant's highly anticipated Hades 2 launches on September 25. The developer has now shared the exact release times so you can jump in the minute the game goes live on its release date.Hades 2 1.0 launches worldwide at 9 AM PDT on September 25 across Switch 2, Switch 1, Steam, and the Epic Games Store. Hades 2 is a console exclusive for Switch at launch and will presumably come to PlayStation and Xbox sometime down the track, but Supergiant hasn't announced when that could be.Hades 2 launch times (all platforms)September 259 AM PDT12 PM EDT1 PM BRT5 PM BST6 PM CEST7 PM EESTSeptember 26Midnight HKT1 AM JST2 AM AESTHades 2 has spent just over a year in early access, adding a substantial amount of content and fine-tuning the gameplay across a number of major updates since May 2024. The game resurfaced at the September Nintendo Direct when Supergiant announced the release date and shared a new trailer. For Switch 2, in TV mode Hades 2 will run at 1080p at 120fps. In handheld mode, it'll support 1080p at 60fps. A physical edition for Switch 2 launches on November 20.Hades 2 costs $30 and is Supergiant's first-ever sequel. The company previously made the games Bastion, Transistor, and Pyre. Released in 2020, Hades was a major success, and the sequel, Hades 2, entered early access back in 2024.
