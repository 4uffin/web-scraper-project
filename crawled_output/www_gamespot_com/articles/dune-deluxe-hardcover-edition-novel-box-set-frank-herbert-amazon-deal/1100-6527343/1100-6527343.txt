Frank Herbert's Dune Saga: 3-Book Deluxe Hardcover Box Set
$65.59 (was $150)
Published in 2023 in anticipation of Dune: Part Two's theatrical release, the Dune Saga Deluxe Edition Box Set bundles Frank Herbert's original trilogy in one stunning collection. Each of the three hardcover books--Dune, Dune: Messiah, and Children of Dune--have stained page edges, foil stamped covers, illustrated endpapers, and full-color artwork on the interior of the dust jacket.The three books are packaged inside a slipcase with original full-color artwork shown above. You can take a closer look at the Dune Deluxe Edition books below. All three books are available individually, too. Each one carries a $50 retail price, but they are on sale for over 50% off, too. The box set is only a couple bucks less than the combined price of the three books, but you'd miss out on the gorgeous slipcase.
