Amazon has opened preorders for an exclusive 4K Blu-ray edition of The Long Walk. Priced at $35, The Long Walk Limited Edition Steelbook is currently the No. 1 best-selling movie on Amazon. Considering the low price, the film's popularity, and the cool interactive artwork featuring Mark Hamill, Stephen King fans may want to secure their preorder soon. It wouldn't be surprising if The Long Walk's Steelbook Edition sold out. It's also possible the price will jump, as the 4K Steelbook is currently five bucks cheaper than the 1080p Blu-ray edition.The dystopian thriller is one of the highest-rated Stephen King movie adaptations of all time from a critical perspective. At the time of writing, The Long Walk holds a 90% on Rotten Tomatoes. Directed by Francis Lawrence and written by JT Mollner, The Long Walk's ensemble cast includes Cooper Hoffman, David Jonsson, Judy Greer, and Mark Hamill, among others.The Long Walk Limited Edition Steelbook | Amazon ExclusiveThe Long Walk is the third King adaptation to hit theaters in 2025, and the first of the three with a Steelbook Edition. Speaking of firsts, you can also preorder the first standalone hardcover edition of the 1979 novel at Amazon. Take a closer look at physical editions of the new adaptation and the classic King novel below.
The Long Walk Limited Edition Steelbook (4K Blu-ray)
$35 | Amazon Exclusive
The Long Walk's Steelbook Edition comes with 4K Blu-ray, 1080p Blu-ray, and digital versions of the movie. The steelbook cases features Mark Hamill's villainous character, the Major, on the front and dog tags of contestants on the back. A transparent sleeve alters the Major's sunglasses. With the sleeve on, you can see the young men walking in the reflection. When the sleeve is removed, the reflection disappears. The interior artwork shows the contestants walking with a tank following behind them.Special features and technical specs haven't been unveiled, but you can expect behind-the-scenes content as well as support for HDR and surround sound.The Long Walk Editions:4K Blu-ray Steelbook -- $35Blu-ray -- $40DVD -- $30

Stephen King - The Long Walk - First Hardcover Edition
$29 | Releases October 7
If you want to read Stephen King's original novel before or after watching the film adaptation, Scribner is publishing a new hardcover edition of The Long Walk on October 7. New editions of King's books are often printed when an adaptation is made, but most of the time these are paperbacks. For big King fans who want to collect hardcover editions, the upcoming edition of The Long Walk is a big deal.This is the first standalone hardcover edition of The Long Walk sold at bookstores and major retailers. The reason for this is because The Long Walk is one of a handful of novels King wrote under the pen name Richard Bachman. Novels published under the Bachman name were only available as paperbacks. The Long Walk hit stores in 1979, but it's actually the first novel King wrote.When the author revealed the true identity of Bachman in 1985, The Long Walk and three other books initially published under the pseudonym were collected in hardcover as The Bachman Books. The Long Walk also received a pricey Limited Edition from Centipede Press in 2023.Earlier this month, Scribner published a paperback tie-in edition with The Long Walk movie poster as the cover art. Stephen King Movies on 4K Blu-rayStephen King movies - 2025 4K Blu-ray releasesAs mentioned, The Long Walk is the first 2025 King adaptation with a Steelbook Edition, but we'd be surprised if The Running Man doesn't get one. The two 2025 adaptations without Steelbook Editions are The Life of Chuck and The Monkey. Directed by Osgood Perkins, The Monkey is a horror-comedy about a cursed toy monkey. It received a standard 4K Blu-ray release in June, and Amazon has it for only $19.67 (was $43) right now. The Life of Chuck, meanwhile, is a fantasy drama directed by Mike Flanagan. The 4K Blu-ray edition releases September 30 and is available to preorder for $27.49 (was $40) at Amazon.Several older film adaptations of King's work have received new 4K Blu-ray editions this year. The Dead Limited Edition Steelbook released in June as an Amazon exclusive; The Dark Half was finally restored in 4K in July; and Creepshow 2 arrives on 4K Blu-ray later this month as a Limited Edition from Arrow Video. The 1990 film Graveyard Shift also released on 4K Blu-ray this year, but unlike the other movies we mentioned, Graveyard Shift is pretty bad.We put together a list of Stephen King movies on 4K Blu-ray. There are numerous stellar deals at Amazon right now, including The Shining for only $11.49 and the sequel Doctor Sleep for $13.
