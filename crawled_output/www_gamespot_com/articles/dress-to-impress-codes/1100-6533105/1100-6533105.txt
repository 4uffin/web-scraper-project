Keep an eye out for Dress to Impress events, where you can take on Halloween themes and story-driven quests. You can choose clothes, accessories, makeup, and even nail color to match the theme as closely as possible before showcasing the finished look on the catwalk. The other players in your lobby will then vote on how closely your aesthetic matches the prompt with a rating out of five stars. The top three entries will then take their place on the podium, rising through the ranks.
