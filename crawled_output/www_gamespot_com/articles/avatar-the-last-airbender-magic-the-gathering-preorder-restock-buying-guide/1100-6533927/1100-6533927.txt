Steven Petite
on September 22, 2025 at 11:35AM PDT 1 Comments
Avatar: The Last Airbender Play Booster Box
$209.70 | Restocked September 22
Preorder at Amazon
Most of the products in Magic: The Gathering's Avatar: The Last Airbender crossover set have been sold out since preorders opened August 13. If you weren't able to secure preorders at that time, you have another chance to preorder the Avatar: The Last Airbender Play Booster Box at Amazon. The retailer restocked the 30-pack box (420 cards total) on September 22. The Play Booster Box is one of three Avatar MTG products Amazon has in stock. The other in-stock preorders are the Beginner Box and the Jumpstart Booster Box. The Avatar: The Last Airbender crossover MTG set is scheduled to launch November 21. Amazon has also restocked the Marvel's Spider-Man Play Booster Box for $180 (was $209.70) ahead of its release this Friday, September 26.
