Switch: $60 | PS5/Xbox: $70
See at Amazon
Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds is available now on Nintendo Switch, PlayStation, Xbox, and PC. The new arcade racer is earning pretty great reviews from critics, as it currently holds an 83 average on Metacritic. CrossWorlds has the largest roster in series history, with a long list of Sonic the Hedgehog characters being joined by famous faces from Minecraft, Pac-Man, SpongeBob SquarePants, Persona, Like A Dragon, and Hatsune Miku. If you want to dive into the most customizable and content-rich Sonic racing game yet, the Amazon-exclusive physical edition is in stock for Switch, PS5, and Xbox.

Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds for Nintendo Switch
$60 | Upgrade to Switch 2 for $10
Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds for Nintendo Switch is 10 bucks cheaper on Nintendo Switch. This version is playable on Switch 2 out of the box.It's worth noting that Sega is developing a dedicated version for Switch 2, but it won't be available until the holiday season in digital format. A true physical edition for Switch 2--the full game on a 64GB game card--will arrive in early 2026.
