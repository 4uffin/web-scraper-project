How To Get The Beaver's Stick Secret Weapon In Dying Light: The BeastBy
Jason Rodriguez
on September 23, 2025 at 11:15AM PDT

Are you curious as to how you'd be able to find the Beaver's Stick in Dying Light: The Beast? This is a secret weapon that's neither tracked as an side quest nor as a collectible. Instead, you have to go off the beaten path, searching for scattered logs and branches. Our guide has all the details that you need to help you obtain this reward.How to get the Beaver's Stick in Dying Light: The Beast - Beaver branches guideThe Beaver's Stick legendary weapon can be yours once you've found all six Beaver Branches in Castor Woods. These materials allow you to repair the dam in the northwestern part of the region. From there, you have to contend with a certain foe to claim your prize.Share this GalleryLatest Image GalleriesHades 2 - All Keepsakes And How To Unlock ThemHades 2 - How To Unlock Animal Familiars And PetsHades 2 Arcana Guide And The Best Arcana Cards To UseBeaver's Stick and Beaver Branches - Map locationsThe map above shows the locations of the items that you need to acquire the Dying Light: The Beast Beaver's Stick legendary weapon. The dam is far to the west of the Industrial Park area. The branches, meanwhile, can be found in various places in Castor Woods. We've organized our guide to primarily focus on the items that are closest to the dam.Note: Once you've arrived at the exact spot, be sure to continuously press the Survivor Sense button. These materials have a bright yellow glow, making them easier to spot.Beaver Branch location #1The first Beaver Branch in Dying Light: The Beast is actually right next to the dam itself. Climb on the rocks to pick it up.Beaver Branch location #2You don't have to travel very far to find this item. Just keep going south and look under the bridge.Beaver Branch location #3This branch is directly south of the Hardware Store dark zone. There's also an abandoned car by the river, and it has a note for the Hardware Store safe. The code is 62-25-33.Beaver Branch location #4From the previous location, move along a straight line heading directly southeast. You should find yourself by the riverbank of the appropriately named Beaver Creek. The item is next to the body of water.Beaver Branch location #5You're going to need more of these items to obtain the Dying Light: The Beast Beaver's Stick. For this one, make your way to St. Valentine Asylum at the outskirts of Old Town. Check the creek area along the western portion of the island.Beaver Branch location #6Last but not least, there's another Beaver Branch under a bridge. It's slightly north of the Abandoned House safe zone.How to claim the Beaver's Stick from the secret caveReturn to the dam after finding all six Beaver Branches in Dying Light: The Beast. Here's what you need to do next:Look for interaction prompts so you can place the branches and repair the dam.Find a safe spot/safe zone and rest until the next day.When you go back to the dam, you'll notice that the set of logs leaning on the base of the hill have been removed.Swim underwater and go through the hidden passageway. You can keep using your Survivor Sense to ping what's in front of you.Once you resurface, you'll find yourself in a cave, but there's definitely something creepy going on here.Use the sofa to rest until the morning.Kyle Crane will suddenly get startled from his slumber when a zombie in a beaver costume--a "zom-beaver" perhaps--barges in. Take it out and pick up the Beaver's Stick from the workbench to claim your reward. It has the following quirks:+500% damage from parkour attacks+6% damage against infected+100% knockdown chance160 durability - This item cannot be repaired once it's broken, but you automatically receive a blueprint so you can craft it again.That does it for our guide on how to get the Beaver's Stick in Dying Light: The Beast. It's not the strongest equipment in the game, as it's mostly an Easter egg of sorts--i.e. a reference to Polish beavers and memes--but it's still worth having in your arsenal.If you're keen on finding other amazing items, you can check our EXPCalibur long sword guide.
