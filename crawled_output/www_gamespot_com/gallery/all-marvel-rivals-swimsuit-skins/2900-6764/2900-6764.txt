All Swimsuit Skins In Marvel Rivals Summer Event 2025By
James Carr
on September 19, 2025 at 10:37AM PDT
GameSpot may receive revenue from affiliate and advertising partnerships for sharing this content and from purchases through links.
The second batch of Marvel Rivals Swimsuit skins has arrived via the Summer Special Vol. 2 update, alongside two new shop skins. In total, this drop of new cosmetics brings five more skins to the game, as well as new recolors for the five swimsuit skins introduced in Season 3. The previous five swimsuit skins for Luna Snow, Psylocke, The Thing, Loki, and Thor, are no longer available in the shop.It was previously announced that Marvel Rivals would be getting swimsuit skins alongside the release of the Swimsuit Special comic book. The new batch of skins comes via the Summer Special Vol 2 event pass, which is available for the next 28 days. If you buy the premium event pass, which costs 990 Lattice ($10 USD), you can continue to unlock rewards on the event pass after it's no longer available for purchase. The event pass includes three skins: Sunny Sizzler Human Torch, Rocky Tide The Thing, and Oceanic Harmony Mantis. The Thing skin is available for free to everyone, while the other two require the premium pass.The other two new skins are available in the shop, set to rotate out after 28 days, just like the new event pass. The skins available in the shop are Azure Shade Invisible Woman and Aqua Arsenal The Punisher. Both skins come bundled with highlight intros, emotes, sprays, and nameplates. You can get a bundle of both skins and all related cosmetics for 3,200 Units ($32 USD) or you can get either skin bundle individually, both priced at 2,200 Units ($22). All five skins have recolors available, which can be purchased with Unstable Molecules, a currency earned in-game or purchased with real money.Season 4 of Marvel Rivals introduced Angela to the roster, with Daredevil joining later for the midseason update.
