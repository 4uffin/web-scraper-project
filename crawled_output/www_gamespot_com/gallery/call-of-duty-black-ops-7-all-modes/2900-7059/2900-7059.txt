Call Of Duty: Black Ops 7 - All The Multiplayer Modes For Beta And LaunchBy
Eddie Makuch
on September 22, 2025 at 9:09AM PDT
GameSpot may receive revenue from affiliate and advertising partnerships for sharing this content and from purchases through links.
Multiplayer mayhemOn September 22, Activision opened the floodgates in terms of new information about Call of Duty: Black Ops 7's multiplayer mode. The company revealed lots and lots, including all the modes at launch.We're rounding up all the modes in this gallery, so be sure to click through the slides to find out everything you'll be able to play. Of course, series favorites like Team Deathmatch and Domination are returning, but there are new modes, too, like Overload.Some of the maps and modes will be available to play during the upcoming multiplayer beta, which begins on October 2. The full game, meanwhile, launches on November 14.For more: see all the Black Ops 7 multiplayer maps.Share this GalleryLatest Image GalleriesJump Space Tips - 8 Things You Need To Know Before You PlayAll Call Of Duty: Black Ops 7 Guns, Weapons, And Perks Available At LaunchAll Call Of Duty: Black Ops 7 Maps Available At LaunchOverload A new mode for Call of Duty, Overload has teams fighting to posses an "Overload Device." A 6v6 mode with respawns, Overload begins with players rushing to grab the Overload Device. Once they have it, the team possessing it must protect the carrier as they push to a place it designated zone near the opposing side's spawn point.The device is always marked on the HUD and Tac-Map, and the player holding the device gets a bonus in the form of personal radar to see where people are. Overload will be available in the Black Ops 7 multiplayer beta.Team DeathmatchKill enemies to score points until the limit is reached. You know what to expect. Team Deathmatch is also available in the beta.Domination Teams capture, hold, and defend objectives. Also available in the beta.Search and Destroy Teams try to detonate and defuse bombs. There are no respawns.Kill Confirmed Players must recover dog tags to score points. This mode will be available in the beta.Free-For-AllPlayers must defeat enemies to rack up points. This is basically Team Deathmatch with no teams.Hardpoint Teams capture and hold a designated Hardpoint to earn score. This mode will be available in the beta.Gunfight Players must take down enemies using pre-determined classes. There is no respawning.Kill Order Players must take down the enemy High Value Target (HVT) while keeping their own HVT alive.Control Teams take turns attacking and defending zones with a limited number of lives per round.Skirmish Skirmish is Call of Duty's large-scale mode, and it's coming back for Black Ops 7. This is a 20v20 mode featuring big maps and new ways to move around like wingsuits and grappling hooks, along with vehicles.Training Course A tutorial of sorts, Training Course is a beginner-themed mode that teaches players the basics. The mode also helps players get up to speed with some of Black Ops 7's new mechanics, like wall-jumping. This mode will be available in the beta. Private matches Black Ops 7 also supports private matches, allowing players to create their own games with their own custom rules. Players can set up private matches with other humans or bots.Alternate ModesIn addition to all the modes mentioned so far, Black Ops 7 will have "alternate modes" that provide a twist on the standard setup.These are listed below, with descriptions provided by Activision.Face Off DominationCapture, hold and defend objectives to earn score for your team. Scorestreaks disabled.Face Off Team DeathmatchEliminate enemy players to earn score for your team. Scorestreaks disabled.Face Off Kill OrderEliminate the enemy HVT while keeping your HVT alive. HVT kills earn bonus score for your team. Scorestreaks disabled.Face Off Kill ConfirmedRecover dog tags to score for your team and deny enemy scores. Scorestreaks disabled.
